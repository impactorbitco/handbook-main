## We've Got You Covered

Clear benefits, real support  

At GitLab, we believe team member benefits should be simple to understand and there when you need them. From life's big moments to everyday questions, our comprehensive coverage gives you clarity and confidence so you can focus on your work and life.  

In addition to the local benefits listed below, all Ireland team members have access to the [company-wide benefits](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/) available to all team members globally.  

## Health & Wellbeing

### Private Medical Insurance  

GitLab provides comprehensive private medical coverage through VHI, giving you access to quality medical treatments, health screenings, and protection against high medical costs. We cover 100% of the premiums for you and your eligible dependents.  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Eligibility Requirements</summary>

- All full-time team members  
- Coverage includes:

  - Team member's spouse  
  - Domestic partner  
  - Children up to age 21 (if enrolled in school). Please make sure to certify that your dependent is still enrolled in school when enrolling them. To do so, members need to sign up using the Online Enrolment portal and there will be a prompt to confirm Yes or No if they are a Student, there is no further details provided at this point as VHI will accept the members answer upon application.

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Coverage & Key Features</summary>

- Access to private medical care and treatments  
- Comprehensive health screenings  
- 100% premium coverage by GitLab  
- [Plan details (PMI 4316)](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Snm1P04QmmaWKo7iOBHGcf0byi3kV7nS/view?usp=sharing)  
- [Benefits brochure](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1s2Wa8K1tJm9E720ml5r1eJehjkQjIx-p/view?usp=sharing)
- [2025 Renewal Webinar Recording](https://drive.google.com/file/d/131qnbKWvYcZbCHYWDFEv86P6THEGgY98/view?usp=sharing)  

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Lifetime Community Rating (LCR)</summary>

From 1st of May 2015, if you are 35 or over when you first take out private health insurance, you will pay an additional amount of 2% for each year that you are aged 35 and over. Please contact VHI if you think this may apply.  
</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Enroll</summary>

1. Visit the [VHI online portal](https://www.vhi.ie/groups/?token=1EFD9A4A7D274CF193FDD729F3AFC145) or call VHI
2. Enter your GitLab Employee ID as "Employee Number"  
3. Enter your GitLab Department (from Workday) as "Cost Centre"
4. Choose PMI 4316 or call 056 777 5800 to discuss your needs

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Important Dates</summary>

- Annual renewal: November 1  
- New hires can enroll from hire date  
- Policy auto-renews annually

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Contact Information</summary>

- VHI Corporate Advice Line: 056 777 5800  
- [Hospital Directory](https://vhi.ie/downloads)

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Tax Considerations</summary>

Health insurance is treated as a Benefit in Kind under Revenue Commissioner rules. Your normal tax rate applies. [Learn more about tax implications](https://www.revenue.ie/en/personal-tax-credits-reliefs-and-exemptions/health-and-age/medical-insurance-premiums/index.aspx).

</details>  

### Eye Care  

Under the Safety, Health, and Welfare at Work Regulations 2007, GitLab provides eye care benefits for team members.  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Coverage & Key Features</summary>

- Regular eye and eyesight exam reimbursement  
- Up to €140 reimbursement for computer glasses (if medically required for VDU use only)  
- Submit VHI claims first, then remaining costs through Navan

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Claim</summary>

1. Get eye exam and prescription
2. Submit eligible costs through VHI first
3. Submit remaining receipts via Navan and email the prescription to total-rewards@gitlab.com

</details>  

## Financial Security

### Pension Plan

GitLab offers a defined contribution pension scheme through Aviva, administered by NFP Ireland, designed to help you build financial security for retirement. Effective 2025-11-01, all new hires will be automatically enrolled under our company plan.

<details markdown="1">
<summary> NEW (January 2026) - Pension Auto Enrolment Regulation </summary>

- Starting January 1, 2026, Ireland's new Auto Enrolment pension scheme (called "My Future Fund") becomes mandatory. All eligible employees who are not already contributing to a workplace pension scheme will be automatically enrolled in this government-run pension program.
- **Opportunity**: Team Members can join our superior company DC pension plan and avoid being automatically enrolled in the less beneficial government scheme.
- **Key Advantages of our Company Plan**: 5% employer contribution (compared to 1.5% employer contribution in Auto Enrolment's first phase), complete contribution flexibility - contribute any amount 1% and above and still be exempt from Auto Enrolment - (compared to mandatory 1.5% employee contribution), Income tax relief on your contributions, reducing your actual cost, Flexible investment options with professional fund management, No mandatory waiting periods or restricted opt-out windows.
- See the [Ireland Auto Enrolment Pension Transition FAQ](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1m2wG90cXvKGB7IK4IR22sbfp5GzTntZw/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=108025801343696146787&rtpof=true&sd=true) for detailed information

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary> GitLab Pension Scheme Coverage </summary>

- New hires are automatically enrolled under the Aviva plan, with a 1.5% Employee and Employer contribution
- Team members can increase their contribution and GitLab matches up to 5% of annual base salary
- Option for team members to make Additional Voluntary Contributions (AVCs)
- Professional administration through NFP Ireland
- Transfers accepted from other pension plans  
- See the [Pension information video](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1i5ir2xMWMIQznq-tEGCmTkSfZ_GuuV46ARlKhAGYrS0/edit)

</details>

3. Email to employeebenefits@nfpireland.ie from your GitLab email

</details>

### Disability Coverage  

Full-time team members receive disability coverage providing 66% of base salary (less social welfare) to age 65.  

Ireland's statutory sick leave policy offers a floor level of protection and does not interfere with existing, more favorable, sick pay schemes such as GitLab's [Sick Time policy](/handbook/people-group/time-off-and-absence/time-off-types/). When allowed by law, GitLab's Sick Leave policy will run concurrently with this leave policy.

- 66% of base salary coverage  
- 13-week deferred period  
- Coverage to age 65  
- [Claim form](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1pHwaBoc0raokcgVsInJeE6QqhVa22XtF/view?usp=sharing)  
- [Early intervention services](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ESvfiqTa6Gw3OsvMvgXrykgUZA80QImn/view?usp=sharing)

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to File a Claim</summary>

1. Notify total-rewards@gitlab.com
2. Complete claim documentation
3. Access support services as needed

</details>

## Growing Family Leaves  

### Maternity Leave  

GitLab provides comprehensive maternity leave support that goes beyond statutory requirements.  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Statutory Entitlement</summary>

- 26 weeks ordinary maternity leave  
- Additional 16 weeks unpaid leave available  
- Must start 2 weeks before due date  
- Minimum 4 weeks after birth

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>GitLab Support</summary>

- 100% salary for up to 16 weeks (includes state benefit top-up)  
- State Maternity Benefit: €262/week (subject to PRSI)  
- Support throughout your leave journey

</details>  

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Apply</summary>

1. Notify leaves@gitlab.com with start/end dates
2. Receive completed MB2 form from Absence Management
3. Apply via [gov.ie](https://www.gov.ie/en/service/apply-for-maternity-benefit/) at least 6 weeks before leave
4. Submit time off in Workday (Parental Leave category)

</details>
