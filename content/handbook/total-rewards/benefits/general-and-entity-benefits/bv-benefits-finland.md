## Health & Wellbeing

GitLab provides preventative occupational healthcare through [Mehiläinen](https://www.mehilainen.fi/en) to keep you healthy and supported throughout your career.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Eligibility & Access</summary>

## Financial Security

### Pension Insurance

GitLab administers pension insurance through [ELO](https://www.elo.fi/en/about-elo#:~:text=We%20are%20Elo%2C%20a%20large,funds%20profitably%2C%20securely%20and%20responsibly.) to secure your financial future. Both you and GitLab contribute to ensure you're building toward retirement from day one.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Contribution Rates & Eligibility</summary>

**Eligibility:** All Finland team members

**Contribution Rates:**

- Ages 17-52: 7.15% (employee) + 17.40% (employer)
- Ages 53-62: 8.65% (employee) + 17.40% (employer)
- Ages 63-67: 7.15% (employee) + 17.40% (employer)

Contributions are automatically handled through payroll.

</details>

### Group Life & Accident Insurance

Comprehensive life and accident coverage through [IF](https://www.if-insurance.com/large-enterprises/insurance-solutions/employee-benefits/employee-benefits-finland) Insurance.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Coverage Details</summary>

**Eligibility:** All Finland team members from hire date

**Coverage:** Automatic enrollment with professional claims support

</details>

### Unemployment Insurance

GitLab handles unemployment insurance contributions for your future financial security.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Rates & Eligibility</summary>

**Eligibility:** Team members aged 17-64

**Rates (2022):** 1.50% employee contribution, automatically deducted through payroll

</details>

## Family & Life Transitions

### Annual Holiday Leave

Holiday entitlement grows with your tenure to ensure you have time to recharge.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Holiday Entitlement</summary>

**Entitlement:**

- Less than 12 months service: 2 days/month totaling 2 weeks
- 12+ months service: 2.5 days/month totaling 30 days per year
- All team members are allowed to carry over up to 24 workdays into the next calendar year, but the balance expires 1 year from the accrual on April 1st annually.

**How to Use:** Request through standard PTO process

</details>

### Family Leave - Supporting Life's Biggest Moments

#### New System (Babies due September 4, 2022 or later)

*Visit [Kela's pregnancy](https://www.kela.fi/during-pregnancy) and [families](https://www.kela.fi/families) pages for details.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Pregnancy Leave</summary>

- 40 working days from Kela
- Start 14-30 days before due date

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Parental Leave</summary>

- Each parent: 160 days (63 transferable)
- Single parents: 320 days total
- Multiple births: +84 days per additional child
- Take in up to 4 periods before child turns 2

</details>

#### Previous System (Babies due before September 4, 2022)

*Visit [Kela's families page](https://www.kela.fi/families) for details.*

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Previous Leave Types</summary>

**Maternity Leave:** 105 working days

**Paternity Leave:** Up to 54 working days (18 can overlap with maternity)

**Parental Leave:** 158 working days after maternity

**Adoption Leave:** 233 days from child's birth

</details>

**How to Request Family Leave:**

1. Select Parental Leave in Workday
2. Absence Management team will contact you
3. GitLab's [16-week policy](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave) runs concurrently when allowed

Payment based on annual income. Find rates at [www.kela.fi/web/en](https://www.kela.fi/web/en).

## Health Support

### Sick Leave

9 days of sick leave, runs concurrently with GitLab's global Sick Time policy. Plus Finland's sickness allowance system for extended illness.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Sick Leave Details</summary>

**Key Features:**

- 9 days paid sick leave, runs concurrently with GitLab's global Sick Time policy
- Kela sickness allowance after 9 working days
- Partial allowance available for part-time work during recovery

**How to Use:**

1. Up to 9 days: Mark "Out Sick" in Workday
2. Over 9 days: Contact [leaves@gitlab.com](mailto:leaves@gitlab.com)
3. Sickness allowance: Apply with Kela at [www.kela.fi/web/en](https://www.kela.fi/web/en)

</details>

## Additional Leave Types

Contact [leaves@gitlab.com](mailto:leaves@gitlab.com) for any of these leave types:

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Additional Leave Types</summary>

**Child Care Leave:** Unpaid leave after parental leave until child turns 3

**Study Leave:** Up to 2 years over 5-year period after 1 year employment

**Job Alternation Leave:** 100-180 days with 20+ years employment history

**Partial Care Leave:** Reduced hours (6/day or 30/week) until child finishes 2nd grade

**Temporary Child Care Leave:** 4 days for sudden child illness (under age 10)

**Family Care Leave:** Flexible arrangements for urgent family medical needs

**Government Duty Leave:** Time off for municipal government positions

**Natural Event Leave:** Up to 14 days paid for uncontrollable events (fires, disasters)

</details>
