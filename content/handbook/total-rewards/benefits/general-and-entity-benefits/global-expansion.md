**Vacation Bonus**

Team members receive additional cash compensation to support their vacation expenses.

- **Bonus Calculation** The vacation bonus equals a minimum of 25% of your daily salary multiplied by the number of vacation days taken.
- **Eligibility Requirements** You become eligible for the vacation bonus after completing one full year of service.

For complete details and requirements, please consult the [government website.](https://www.gob.mx/profedet/articulos/la-ley-federal-del-trabajo-es-tu-aliada-en-tu-derecho-a-recibir-vacaciones?idiom=es#:~:text=Durante%20el%20periodo%20de%20vacaciones%20el%20patr%C3%B3n%20deber%C3%A1,que%20les%20correspondan%20durante%20el%20per%C3%ADodo%20de%20vacaciones)

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Social Security Benefits</summary>

All workers in Mexico must be registered with and contribute to social security institutions under the Social Security Law.

**Key Institutions**

- **Mexican Institute of Social Security (IMSS):** Administers medical services, child day care, accident and sickness compensation, pregnancy benefits, and disability pensions
- **National Workers' Housing Fund Institute (INFONAVIT):** Provides subsidized housing, loans, and the Retirement Savings Program (SAR), which offers retirement benefits at age 65

**Contribution Requirements** Both employers and employees are required to contribute to social security, with employers responsible for withholding employee contributions. These contributions fund retirement pensions, health and maternity insurance, occupational risk coverage, day-care, disability and life insurance, and unemployment/old age insurance.

<details markdown="1">
<summary>UAE Leave Policies</summary>

**Annual Leave**

- **After 1 Year:** 30 calendar days/22 working days
- **First Year:** 2 days per month after 6 months of service
- **Part-Time:** Prorated based on actual working hours

**Parental Leave**

Private sector team members are entitled to 5 paid working days of parental leave, available to both mothers and fathers. This leave can be taken from the day of the child's birth up to six months after delivery.

**Maternity Leave**

Female workers receive 60 days of maternity leave structured as follows:

- **45 days:** Fully paid leave
- **15 days:** Half-paid leave (during probation period only)

**Application Timing:** To initiate parental leave, submit your time off by selecting parental leave category in [Workday - Time Off](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ZatJdGp485lOS2QVrxYJByQK8dD_CMGiLVOykgd338w/edit) at least 30 days before the leave starts. Please familiarize yourself with GitLabs [Parental leave policy](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave).

**Additional Maternity Provisions:**

- **Pregnancy/childbirth complications:** Additional 45 days unpaid leave available if unable to return to work due to illness resulting from pregnancy or childbirth. Medical certificate from respective medical authority required. Days may be taken consecutively or intermittently.
- **Sick or disabled child:** Additional 30 days fully paid leave, extendable for another 30 days unpaid. Medical certificate from respective medical authority required to prove child's illness or disability.
- **Nursing breaks:** After returning to work, entitled to one or two daily breaks for nursing (maximum one hour total per day) for 6 months following delivery. These breaks are fully paid.

**Sabbatical Leave (National Service)**

Under UAE Labour Law, Emirati citizens working in the private sector are entitled to paid sabbatical leave to perform national service.

**Sick Leave**

**Eligibility:** Available after completing the probationary period. Maximum 90 days per year (continuous or intermittent).

**Payment Structure:**

- **First 15 days:** Full pay
- **Next 30 days:** Half pay
- **Remaining 45 days:** No pay

**Probationary Period:** Sick leave available without pay, subject to employer approval and medical report confirming necessity.

**Notification Requirements:** Must notify employer within 3 days of illness and submit medical report from medical entity (per Article 31 of UAE Labour Law).

**Ineligibility Situations:**

- During probation period (for paid leave)
- Illness resulting from employee misconduct (alcohol/narcotics consumption)
- Illness resulting from violation of safety instructions per UAE legislation and company regulations

**Note:** Above provisions do not apply to occupational illness-related sick leave.

**Special Leave**

- **Bereavement:** 5 days (spouse), 3 days (parent/child/sibling/grandparent/grandchild)
- **Hajj:** Up to 30 days unpaid, once per employment period
- **National Service:** Paid sabbatical for Emiratis

**Application Process**

**For Parental Leave:** Submit through Time Off by Deel, select Parental Leave label. Absence Management team will provide additional information.

**For All Leave Types:** Submit requests via Workday - Time Off at least 30 days before leave starts. Please review GitLab's Parental Leave policy for complete details.
