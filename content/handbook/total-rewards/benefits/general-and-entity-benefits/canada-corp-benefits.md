## Health & Wellbeing Coverage

### Medical, Dental, and Vision Coverage

Comprehensive health coverage through ManuLife with no cost to you. GitLab covers 100% of premiums for team members, spouses, dependents, and domestic partners.

#### Eligibility

- All GitLab Canada Corp team members
- Coverage effective from hire date
- Spouses, dependents, and domestic partners eligible

#### Key Features

- Complete medical, dental, and vision coverage at no cost
- Separate plans for Quebec ([Plan B- English](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xCtEk8bHHCkwdlQWeNBfTdQ7g-p1cSVM/view?usp=sharing) & [Plan B- French](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xCtEk8bHHCkwdlQWeNBfTdQ7g-p1cSVM/view?usp=sharing)) and other provinces ([Plan A- English](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xCtEk8bHHCkwdlQWeNBfTdQ7g-p1cSVM/view?usp=sharing) & [Plan A- French](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xCtEk8bHHCkwdlQWeNBfTdQ7g-p1cSVM/view?usp=sharing))
- WorldCare Medical Second Opinion service
- Personalized Prescribing genetic testing reimbursement
- 24/7 TELUS Health Virtual Care in English and French
- Earn Aeroplan Points

#### How to Enroll

1. Choose coverage level (individual or family)
2. Email decision to total-rewards@gitlab.com
3. Complete ManuLife registration when invitation arrives
4. Access benefits at [portal.manulife.ca](https://portal.manulife.ca/gb/sponsor-portal/public/welcomePage)

## Financial Security & Retirement

### Registered Retirement Savings Plan (RRSP)

Build your financial future with GitLab's Group RRSP through Canada Life. Employer matching up to 3% of base salary (maximum $2,500 CAD annually).

#### Eligibility

- All full-time team members (30+ hours per week)
- Effective from start date or November 1, 2021 (whichever is later)

#### How to Enroll

1. Visit [mycanadalifeatwork.com](https://mycanadalifeatwork.com)
2. Use [enrollment express PDF](https://drive.google.com/file/d/16K333QPsXllZFolC_yiXZYb3rUYJwXvu/view?usp=sharing) and [video guide](https://www.brainshark.com/1/player/grs?pi=zGWz97lF4zPVigz0&r3f1=c5ff81d2ded38b99c999f69cca80c7ded98198f2dbc5c1cd9e&fb=0)
3. Set contributions: First 3% in "Member" field, additional in "Member Voluntary"
4. Choose beneficiary (separate from life insurance)

#### Important Notes

- Contribution changes allowed anytime (1-2 payroll cycles to reflect)
- You're responsible for tracking personal RRSP contribution limits
- Bonus contributions: Request through [HelpLab](https://helplab.gitlab.systems/esc?id=emp_taxonomy_topic&topic_id=7b018859478c069067429ee0026d4352) before [payroll cutoff](https://internal.gitlab.com/handbook/finance/payroll/?search=payroll)
- Transfers & withdrawals available April 2025

#### Questions?

- **Canada Life**: 1-800-724-3402
- **SC Insurance**: info@scinsurance.ca

## Life & Family Protection

### Life Insurance & AD&D

- **Benefit**: 2x annual earnings (max $500,000)
- **Reductions**: 50% at age 65, terminates at age 71
- **Beneficiaries**: Designate through ManuLife portal using [these instructions](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1P8ekxi7aWF5FxI2wdAkO1dDYSCDEJovm/view?usp=sharing)

### Long Term Disability

- **Waiting Period**: 90 days
- **Benefit**: 66.67% of monthly earnings (max $12,500)
- **Duration**: To age 65 with 2-year own occupation definition

## Parental Leave

GitLab supplements government benefits to provide 100% salary for up to 16 weeks.

### Benefits

- GitLab top-up to reach 100% salary if receiving Employment Insurance
- Full 100% salary if ineligible for Employment Insurance
- Provincial compliance with statutory requirements

<details markdown="1">
<summary>Provincial Entitlements</summary>

**Maternity Leave:**

- Ontario: 17 weeks | Quebec: 18 weeks | BC: 17 weeks | Alberta: 16 weeks | Manitoba: 17 weeks

**Parental Leave:**

- Ontario: 61-63 weeks | Quebec: 65 weeks | BC: 62 weeks | Alberta: 62 weeks | Manitoba: 63 weeks

</details>

<details markdown="1">
<summary>How to Apply</summary>

1. Select "Parental Leave" in Workday (6+ weeks advance notice)
2. Submit health certificate to leaves@domain.com (if pregnant)
3. Apply for government benefits:
   - Outside Quebec: [Service Canada](https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/programs/ei/ei-list/reports/maternity-parental.html#h2.3)
   - Quebec: [QPIP](https://www.rqap.gouv.qc.ca/en/about-the-plan/general-information/premiums-and-maximum-insurable-earnings)
4. Access ROE through [My Service Canada Account](https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/services/my-account.html)

</details>

## Time Away & Leave

### Paid Time Off & Vacation

GitLab's comprehensive PTO policy provides flexible time off while ensuring compliance with provincial statutory requirements.

**Key Features**

- **Flexible PTO**: No set annual limit - take time off as needed with manager coordination
- **Advance Notice**: Coordinate with manager at least twice as many days in advance as total days requested (e.g., 6 business days notice for 3 days off)
- **Provincial Compliance**: Statutory minimums automatically tracked and managed
- **Usage**: Designate all vacation time as "Paid Time Off" in Workday

**Statutory Vacation Minimums by Province**

- **All Provinces (except SK, QC, ON)**: 2 weeks after 1 year / 3 weeks after 5 years (4% vacation pay first 5 years, 6% after)
- **Quebec**: 2 weeks first year, 3 weeks after 3 years (4% pay first 3 years, 6% after)
- **Saskatchewan**: 3 weeks after 1 year / 4 weeks after 10 years (6% pay first 9 years, 8% after)
- **Ontario**: 2 weeks after 1 year / 3 weeks after 5 years (4% pay first 5 years, 6% after)

**How to Request Time Off**

1. Discuss plans with your manager with appropriate advance notice
2. Coordinate with team to ensure coverage
3. Submit request in Workday using "Paid Time Off" category
4. Set up out-of-office responses and coverage plans

### Sick Leave

**GitLab Canada Corp Entitlement**

- **Annual Allowance**: 10 days per year (3 days available after first month of employment)
- **Usage**: For illness, medical appointments, or caring for sick family members
- **Reporting**: Designate time as "Out Sick" in Workday for proper tracking

**Provincial Statutory Requirements**

- **Ontario**: 3 days job-protected unpaid sick leave (after 2+ weeks employment)
- **Quebec**: 2 days paid absence per calendar year

**Extended Sick Leave (5+ days)**

- GitLab supplements pay up to 100% of regular salary for up to 25 working days
- Additional 25 working days available for family care if not covered by provincial benefits
- Contact Absence Management team at leaves@domain.com
- Medical documentation may be required per local requirements
