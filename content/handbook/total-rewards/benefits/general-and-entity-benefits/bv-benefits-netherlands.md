## Health & Wellbeing

### Medical Allowance

Full-time Netherlands team members receive a EUR 150 gross monthly allowance to help cover healthcare costs.

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Eligibility Requirements</summary>

* Full-time employment status
* Based in the Netherlands

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Coverage & Key Features</summary>

* EUR 150 gross monthly payment via payroll
* No documentation required
* Continues during sick pay or unpaid parental leave
* Prorated for mid-month starts/terminations
* Subject to standard tax withholding

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Important Details</summary>

* You must still purchase your own basisverzekering (basic health insurance) as required by Dutch law
* Not having health insurance can result in fines from the Sociale Verzekeringsbank

</details>

### CZ Collective Healthcare Policy

GitLab partners with [CZ](https://www.cz.nl/zorgverzekering/collectieve-zorgverzekering?015418634) to offer discounted supplementary insurance rates through our collective policy.

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Eligibility Requirements</summary>

* All GitLab Netherlands team members
* Can include dependents

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Coverage & Key Features</summary>

* Discounted rates on supplementary insurance
* Direct payment to CZ via bank account
* Annual enrollment period (end of year)
* Not obligatory \- you can choose any approved provider

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>How to Enroll/Access</summary>

* Review enrollment instructions in the [Netherlands CZ Collective Policy document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1waw2rNECdmsinqqmtj4XNuL0zMQvFeyI17-0YBvKURM/edit)
* Visit the [GitLab CZ page](https://www.cz.nl/zorgverzekering/collectieve-zorgverzekering?015418634)
* Contact CZ directly at 088 555 7777 for questions

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Important Dates</summary>

* Team members can enroll in the plans during the enrollment period at the end of each year
* Team members must cancel their old insurance before January 1 and enroll in new insurance before February 1\. The coverage will be retroactive to January 1
* For more details, review the [Changing Health Insurance](https://www.government.nl/topics/health-insurance/standard-health-insurance/changing-your-health-insurance) page on the Netherlands Government Website

</details>

## Financial Security

### Vacation Money (Vakantiegeld)

Netherlands team members receive the customary vacation money payment each May, as defined by the government.

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Key Features</summary>

* Paid annually in May
* Amount determined by [government regulations](https://www.rijksoverheid.nl/onderwerpen/vakantiedagen-en-vakantiegeld/vraag-en-antwoord/hoe-hoog-is-mijn-vakantiegeld)
* Built into your offer and reflected in the [Compensation Calculator](/handbook/total-rewards/compensation/compensation-calculator/)

* Statutory Sick Leave
  * Team members who are unable to work due to illness are entitled to 70% of their salary for up to 104 weeks. This leave runs concurrently with GitLab's [Sick Time Policy](/handbook/people-group/time-off-and-absence/time-off-types/). Team members will receive 100% of their salary for the first 25 days of illness.
  * GitLab complies with [Dutch local Laws](https://business.gov.nl/regulation/reporting-employee-illness-recovery/) regarding sick leave. Local laws override any leave policy we have in the benefit of the team members.
  * Team members must select `Out Sick` in Workday to ensure that all sick time is properly tracked. For complete reporting procedures, please review [the sick time procedure for The Netherlands here](/handbook/people-group/time-off-and-absence/time-off-types/).
* Statutory Vacation Leave
  * Team members are entitled to at least 20 vacation days per year which run concurrently with GitLab PTO. The days will accrue at 1.67 days/month from the team member's start date. Any unused days will be carried into the next calendar year, but expire on July 1st. Should the team member leave GitLab within the calendar year, an entitlement to a pro rata part vacation days exists. Team members must designate all vacation days taken as `Paid Time OFF` in Workday to ensure that vacation entitlement is properly tracked.

  </details>

### Retirement Plan

Starting January 1, 2021, all team members over 21 receive a private pension plan through [ASR](https://www.asr.nl/zakelijk/pensioen/doenpensioen) (formerly Brand New Day) to supplement the standard state pension (AOW).

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Eligibility Requirements</summary>

* Must be over 21 years old
* Employed via GitLab BV Netherlands contract
* Automatic enrollment upon hire

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Key Features</summary>

* Defined Contribution Agreement with 5% flat rate of pension base
* You contribute 2%, GitLab contributes 3%
* Build invested pension fund for retirement income
* Death benefits for partner and children during service
* Incapacity coverage continues contributions if you're unable to work
* Option for voluntary additional contributions
* Choice of investment profiles: neutral, defensive, offensive, or custom mix

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>How to Enroll/Access</summary>

* Automatically enrolled through our Netherlands benefits partner
* Contact ASR for questions or changes
* Access training videos in the [ASR resource folder](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1qjCicb0JxnCmG5i4l3Hv5CEs5q8mOuXX?usp=sharing)

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Important Details</summary>

* Team members can transfer pension value from former employer within 6 months
* Review the [Pension Scheme document](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1FaC8sHe-r_PHz4xrXbNYlH2zCHAU972h/view?usp=sharing) for complete details
* Learn about voluntary contributions in the [2024 ANW-gap pension guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1vHUp7koRsOMVZk87uMGIpU8QJ0NhRE7x/edit)

</details>

## Growing Families Leaves

16 weeks of fully paid leave to welcome your new child, with flexibility in how you use it.

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Leave Entitlement</summary>

* Up to 6 weeks before estimated birth date
* 10 weeks after birth
* Can reduce pre-birth leave to minimum 4 weeks and add unused days after birth
* 100% salary continuation (GitLab tops up [UWV benefit](https://www.uwv.nl/en/about-uwv/executive-board))
* The statutory leave will run concurrently with [GitLab Parental Leave](/handbook/total-rewards/benefits/general-and-entity-benefits/#parental-leave)

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>How to Access</summary>

* Email [leaves@gitlab.com](mailto:leaves@gitlab.com) with your desired start date
* Can start 6 weeks before, but no later than 4 weeks before due date
* HR Savvy handles UWV application on your behalf
* The benefit is always directly paid to Gitlab so that there is once source of pay‚Äù

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>Important Details</summary>

* If unable to work from 6 weeks before due date, leave automatically begins

</details>

## Time Off Policies

### Sick Leave Support

Comprehensive coverage when you're unable to work due to illness.

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary\>Leave Entitlement</summary>

* 100% salary for first 25 days
* 70% salary continuation for up to 104 weeks total
* GitLab complies with [Dutch local laws](https://business.gov.nl/regulation/reporting-employee-illness-recovery/) regarding sick leave. Local laws override any leave policy we have in the benefit of the team members

</details>

<details markdown="1"\>

<summary>How to Report</summary>

* Select "Out Sick" in Workday

Employees of GitLab B.V. have further rights and responsibilities regarding sick time based on Dutch law, as written into their employment [contracts](https://internal.gitlab.com/handbook/people-group/people-operations/people-operations/employment_contracts/):

* Team members must notify their manager of any sick leave needed.
* Team members must then enter the sick time via Workday as aligned above.
* If the sick leave extends 8 days it should be reported to the Absence Management team via leaves@gitlab.com by the Manager and team member (if able).
* If team members are reporting multiple instances of sick leave in a 28 day timeframe, if this happens, it will be considered the same sickness. Please reach out to the Absence Management team.
* Once the Absence Management team has been notified of the sick time they will inform HRSavvy.
* HRSavvy will be registering the sick time in their HR portal from the first day of absence (retrospectively).
* GitLab has engaged with an occupational health and safety centre, called Leuk Personeel, to assist employees of GitLab B.V. who may need support while being on long-term sick leave.
* If the team members is on long-term sick leave (8 days of more), the Absence Management team will instruct HRSavvy to register the team member at the occupational health and safety service, depending on the situation and agreement with the team member.
* Team members are expected to be available and follow the applicable Act (Gatekeeper Act) and internal policy.

</details>
