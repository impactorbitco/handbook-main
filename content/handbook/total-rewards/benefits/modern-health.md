## Mental Wellness Support

GitLab provides comprehensive mental wellness support through Modern Health to help you navigate life's challenges with confidence. Whether you're managing everyday stress, working through career transitions, or supporting your family's wellbeing, our partnership gives you access to professional coaching, therapy, and digital wellness resources designed to support you at every stage of life.

## Eligibility

- All GitLab team members from day one
- Eligible family members including:
  - Spouse or legal/domestic partner
  - Children up to age 27
  - Partner's children up to age 27

## Key Features

**Professional Support That Fits Your Needs**

- 6 complimentary coaching sessions per year for personal and professional growth
- 6 complimentary therapy sessions per year with licensed mental health professionals
- Unlimited digital wellness programs including guided meditations and self-paced courses
- Live group sessions (Circles) led by Modern Health professionals
- 24/7 access to curated content and resources

**Comprehensive Coverage Areas** Modern Health supports you through work performance, relationships, stress and anxiety management, healthy lifestyles, financial wellbeing, diversity and inclusion, life challenges, and mindfulness practices.

## Understanding Your Options

**Coaching vs. Therapy - What's Right for You?**

**Coaching** focuses on personal and professional development for individuals looking to:

- Develop leadership or communication skills
- Navigate career transitions
- Build healthy habits
- Improve work-life balance

[Learn more about professional coaching](https://app.box.com/s/eaodx06qv9n6cope8koill1wx0onm2d7) in this 42-second video.

**Therapy** provides clinical support from licensed professionals for individuals experiencing:

- Persistent anxiety or depression
- Symptoms affecting daily functioning
- Clinical mental health concerns
- Need for specialized treatment approaches

Not sure which is right for you? The wellbeing assessment will help determine the most appropriate starting point, and you can always request therapy services by contacting help@joinmodernhealth.com.

## Crisis Support

Modern Health is not a crisis resource. For mental health emergencies, contact:

- Emergency services (911 or appropriate local emergency number)
- US: 988 or [988lifeline.org](https://988lifeline.org/)
- US/Canada: [suicidepreventionlifeline.org](https://suicidepreventionlifeline.org/) or 1-800-273-8255
- [Crisis line in your area](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_suicide_crisis_lines)
- Your nearest emergency room
