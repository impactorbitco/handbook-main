**Elevate Applied: Continuous Leadership Development Beyond Certification**

Elevate Applied is a comprehensive post-certification program designed to support leaders who have completed ElevatePrep, Elevate or Elevate+. This program provides ongoing resources, coaching, and learning opportunities to help you continuously develop your leadership skills and apply proven methodologies in your daily work.

*Please note: Elevate Applied is currently under development. Content and offerings will be expanded and refined based on participant feedback and program evolution.*

## Continuous Learning Philosophy

Elevate Applied operates on the principle that leadership development is an ongoing journey, not a destination. The program provides multiple pathways for continued growth:

**Apply** - Put Elevate concepts into practice with real workplace challenges  
**Connect** - Build relationships with other leaders facing similar challenges  
**Develop** - Continuously refine and expand your leadership capabilities  
**Contribute** - Share your learnings and mentor others in their leadership journey

### Professional Coaching

*Please note: The next iteration of our Elevate Applied Coaching offering is under development, more details will be released soon.*

All leaders who have earned their Elevate certification gain access to the following exclusive benefits. Elevate Applied 1:1 Coaching is an opportunity for managers to work 1:1 with GitLab-trained professional coaches on specific skills. Eligibility is open to all *people managers* with priority given to applicants who have completed Elevate/Elevate+. This offering is covered by your Growth and Development Fund as a recommended coaching experience. Unlike other G&D submissions, participants will not cover costs up front; this will be directly billed to GitLab and covered by the G&D allocation for each participant.

**Ongoing 1:1 Leadership Coaching**

- Access to certified professional coaches specializing in leadership development
- Personalized coaching sessions focused on your specific leadership challenges and growth areas
- Integration support to help you apply Elevate concepts in real workplace situations
