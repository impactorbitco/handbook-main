A strategic group of senior leaders who embed DIB strategy into business operations and provide critical insights on divisional imperatives. Members bring diverse capabilities including geographic expertise, division knowledge, and representation from underrepresented groups.

## Responsibilities

1. Embed DIB strategic imperatives into business goals.
2. Unblock obstacles in global DIB strategy implementation.
3. Provide division and geographic insights to the DIB team.
4. Represent DIB in E-group and CEO Skips syncs.
5. Act as DIB Interruptorsâ€”actively advocate, challenge norms, and iterate to keep Diversity, Inclusion and Belonging top of mind.

## Structure & Members

Senior leadership representative (Sr. Dir+) from each department, plus a manager+ from an underrepresented group where Sr. Dir+ URG representation isn't available.

- Engineering - Two Representatives
- Product - Two Representatives
- Sales - Two Representatives
- Marketing - Two Representatives
- Finance - One representative
- Legal & Corporate Affairs - One representative
- Security - One representative
- People - Represented by DIB and the PBPs
- People Business Partners
- Office of CEO representative - One representative
- VP of DIB - Leadership DIB Council Lead
- DIB Team
- Executive Sponsors

## Selection Requirements

- Nominated by E-Group member
- Performing well (not in performance management)
- Full-time team member (including PEO and contractors)
- Minimum 6 months tenure
- Strong understanding of DIB issues and initiatives (TMRGs, Unconscious Bias, Psychological Safety, etc.)
- Ability to advocate for both department needs and DIB strategy

### Process

1. DIB Team adds nominations to E-Group Weekly Meeting agenda
2. E-Group has two weeks to submit nominations to CPO or VP of DIB
3. Nominees must be pre-notified
4. New members receive introduction, mission overview, and accountability briefing
5. Members acknowledge commitments before joining

## Active Participation

- Attend regular sync meetings (AM or PM PST, rotating to accommodate all time zones)
- Participate in discussions, provide insights, and take action items
- Review notes or recordings from missed meetings
- Advocate for and sponsor DIB programs, initiatives, and trainings

## On-Going Engagement

- Contribute asynchronously via Slack, GitLab, or other channels
- Seek and distribute DIB information within your department
- Serve as trusted advisor on DIB issues
- Maintain selection requirements
- Dedicate 2-3 hours monthly, including the monthly meeting

### Duration: Minimum one-year commitment

All DIB council members will be added to our internal [Google Group](https://groups.google.com/a/gitlab.com/g/dib-council) for invitations and communication.
