What Personal Data Does Impact Orbit Collect About Me?

The categories of Personal Data we collect depend on the Services you use and how you engage with us.

We may automatically collect certain information when you use our websites or Services:

Information From Third Parties

We may receive Personal Data about you from:
	•	Vendors and Partners: For example, referrals, partner-provided business contact information, or demographic insights.

## Introduction

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group** (“we”, “our”, “us”), we are committed to protecting your personal data in line with the **UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR)** and the **Data Protection Act 2018**.  

This Privacy Notice explains:

- The Personal Data we collect and process  
- How and why we process Personal Data  
- Your rights under UK GDPR  
- How we share and protect Personal Data  

It applies to all services, platforms, and websites operated by Impact Orbit Creative Group, including:

- `greenorbit.space`  
- `greenorbit.digital`  
- `greenorbit.academy`  
- `leicesterspaceweek.com`

and their subdomains, SaaS platforms, self-managed deployments, and other services (collectively, “Services”).

## Personal Data We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

- **Account Data**: Name, email, username, password, country/region  
- **Profile Data**: Avatar, organization, job title, social media handles, biography  
- **Payment & Verification Data**: Billing info, credit card/bank details, license keys, identity verification  
- **Contact Data**: Address, phone number, company details (when signing up for events, marketing, or support)  
- **Service Content**: Code, repositories, commits, files, AI/ML prompts and outputs, comments, project contributions  
- **Support & Professional Services**: Queries, requests, consultation information  
- **Call Recordings & Webinars**: Audio/video recordings for training and internal review  
- **Other Submissions**: Feedback, surveys, contests, blog posts, interactive content, event participation

### 2. Automatically Collected Data

- **Device & Technical Data**: Browser, OS, IP, hardware identifiers, approximate location  
- **Usage & Subscription Data**: Service usage, license activity, historical engagement  
- **Website Activity**: Page visits, clicks, referrer, de-identified session replay  
- **Cookies & Tracking**: Functional, analytical, and marketing cookies  
- **Email Engagement**: Opens, clicks, interactions

### 3. Third-Party Sources

- **Partners, Vendors, Affiliates**: Business contact info, order history, demographics  
- **Integrations & Services**: Meta, Google, Slack, Jira, GitPod  
- **Other Users**: Colleagues, administrators, or collaborators

### 4. AI-Powered Services

- Code, prompts, and contextual data processed by third-party AI providers  
- Outputs and usage data used for feature improvement and analytics  
- Inputs are **not used to train models without explicit consent**

## Your UK GDPR Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your Personal Data  
- Correct inaccuracies  
- Restrict processing  
- Delete or anonymise data  
- Port your Personal Data to another service  
- Withdraw consent for specific processing  
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests  

### Exercising Your Rights

- **Marketing opt-out**: Use email unsubscribe links or preference center  
- **Interest-based ads opt-out**: See [Cookies Policy](#)  
- **Request a copy of your data**: Submit via Personal Data Request Form  
- **Update profile info**: Log in and edit account settings  
- **Delete account**: Via SaaS “Delete Account” or Personal Data Request Form (corporate accounts require admin action first)  
- **Project porting**: Export via SaaS or clone repositories, API for profile data  
- **Supervisory authority complaints**: File with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at https://ico.org.uk
