This statement is made in accordance with section 414CZA Companies Act 2006 (as amended). In accordance with their duties under section 172(1) Companies Act 2006, the Directors of Green Orbit Digital Ltd. have collectively and individually acted in a way that they consider, in good faith, promotes the success of the Company for the benefit of its members as a whole. In doing so, they have had regard not just to financial factors – denoted by the £ symbol – but also to the factors specified in s.172(1)(a) to (f) Companies Act 2006 (the “Factors”) – denoted by the symbols shown below. The table below explains why the Directors always have regard to these Factors in their decision-making.
Factor: Explanation of why the Directors have regard to this Factor
(a) The likely consequences of any long-term decision
At Green Orbit Digital, our mission is to drive sustainable innovation in space marketing and operations. This is embedded in our long-term Vision to make sustainability a core tenet of the space industry's growth. Decisions are made with the understanding that achieving this Vision requires balancing immediate client needs with long-term environmental and reputational impacts. For further information about how our Vision shapes our Purpose and Values, see page 10 of the Directors' Report.
(b) The interests of the Company’s employees
Our employees are integral to our success. We provide opportunities for growth, training, and a supportive environment that encourages creativity and innovation, particularly in tackling the challenges of sustainable space marketing. Regular engagement sessions, feedback surveys, and team workshops ensure that employee interests and views are central to our decision-making process. See the Directors’ Report on page 13 for more information on employee engagement.
(c) The need to foster business relationships with stakeholders
Green Orbit Digital thrives through strong relationships with clients, partners, and suppliers in the space and sustainability sectors. Collaboration with space startups, established organisations, and environmental bodies is at the heart of our mission to integrate sustainable practices into the industry. By actively engaging with stakeholders, we ensure their perspectives shape our strategies and maintain alignment with our shared goals. See the Directors’ Report on page 15 for an overview of our key stakeholders and engagement activities.
(d) The impact of the Company’s operations on the community and environment
Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond marketing to the broader impacts of space industry operations. We prioritise reducing carbon footprints, preserving the night sky, and advocating for green practices. This includes advising clients on eco-conscious marketing strategies and conducting environmental impact assessments. Decisions are made with careful consideration of how they affect the environment and local communities. See page 17 of the Directors’ Report for details on our environmental initiatives.
(e) Maintaining a reputation of high standards and business conduct
Our reputation as a trusted and sustainable marketing agency underpins our ability to attract and retain clients in the competitive space sector. We hold ourselves to the highest ethical and professional standards, promoting transparency and integrity in all aspects of our business. Maintaining this reputation is crucial to achieving our Vision and fostering trust among our stakeholders.
(f) Acting fairly between members of the Company
As a privately held company, Green Orbit Digital remains accountable to its shareholders. All decisions are made with fairness and transparency, ensuring alignment between the interests of our shareholders and the broader goals of the Company. See page 26 of the Financial Statements for information on financial performance.
This statement highlights our commitment to acting responsibly and in accordance with s.172(1) Companies Act 2006, ensuring the long-term success of Green Orbit Digital Ltd.
