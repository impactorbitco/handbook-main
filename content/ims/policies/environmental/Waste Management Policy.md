## Introduction

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, we recognise the critical importance of responsible waste management in protecting the environment and promoting sustainable business practices. This policy outlines our commitment to reducing waste, enhancing recycling and reuse, and ensuring the safe and responsible disposal of all waste materials generated by our operations.

## Scope

This policy applies to all operations, employees, contractors, and business partners across **Impact Orbit Creative Group** and its subsidiaries.

## Our Commitment

1. **Waste Reduction**  
   We aim to minimize the generation of waste throughout our operations by:  
   - Reducing the use of materials wherever feasible  
   - Prioritising reusable and recyclable materials  
   - Streamlining processes to avoid unnecessary waste  

2. **Recycling and Reuse**  
   We prioritise recycling and the reuse of materials to extend their lifecycle:  
   - Providing accessible recycling facilities for paper, plastics, metals, and other recyclable materials  
   - Encouraging all employees to actively participate in recycling initiatives  
   - Exploring opportunities to reuse materials within our operations  

3. **Responsible Disposal**  
   For waste that cannot be reduced, reused, or recycled, we ensure proper disposal:  
   - Complying with all relevant local, national, and international waste disposal regulations  
   - Using environmentally responsible disposal methods  
   - Partnering with certified and reputable waste management providers  

4. **Awareness and Training**  
   We educate and engage employees in effective waste management:  
   - Conducting regular training sessions on waste reduction, recycling, and disposal best practices  
   - Sharing information on the environmental impacts of waste  
   - Encouraging employee participation and suggestions for waste management improvements  

5. **Continuous Improvement**  
   We are committed to ongoing improvement in our waste management practices:  
   - Regularly reviewing and updating our policies and procedures  
   - Monitoring and reporting on waste management performance  
   - Exploring new methods and technologies for more efficient and sustainable waste management  

## Responsibilities

- **Management**: Ensures the implementation and enforcement of this policy and provides necessary resources  
- **Environmental Management / Sustainability Team**: Oversees waste management initiatives, compliance, and performance tracking  
- **All Employees**: Expected to adhere to this policy and actively contribute to waste reduction, recycling, and responsible disposal  

## Compliance

- All employees must comply with this policy and with applicable local, national, and international waste management regulations  
- Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action or other corrective measures  

## Review and Update

- This policy will be reviewed at least annually and updated as needed to ensure continued relevance and effectiveness  
- Updates will reflect changes in regulations, technology, and best practices  

## Approval

This policy has been approved by the executive management of **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, reflecting our commitment to responsible and sustainable waste management.
