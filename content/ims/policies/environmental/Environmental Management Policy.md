# Environmental Management Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a structured framework for the development, implementation, and continuous improvement of an Environmental Management System (EMS) in compliance with ISO 14001 standards. This policy aims to promote sustainable practices, reduce environmental impact, and align with the strategic direction of the organization.

## Objectives

- To minimize waste generation, energy usage, water usage, and carbon emissions.
- To enhance environmental stewardship among employees and stakeholders.
- To implement cost-effective and environmentally sustainable practices across all operations.
- To contribute to achieving Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), specifically SDG 06 (Clean Water and Sanitation), SDG 07 (Affordable and Clean Energy), and SDG 15 (Life on Land).

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and stakeholders involved in the organization’s operations, projects, and initiatives. It encompasses all activities related to sustainability, environmental responsibility, and compliance with regulatory and ISO standards.

## Key Principles

1. **Sustainability Integration:** Align environmental management practices with the organization’s mission, as outlined in the Integrated Employee Training Programme for Social and Environmental Responsibility.
2. **Compliance and Standards:** Maintain compliance with ISO 14001 standards and other relevant environmental regulations.
3. **Waste Management:** Implement guidelines for waste reduction and adherence to WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) directives.
4. **Energy Efficiency:** Promote energy-efficient practices, leveraging resources such as the Environmental Management System (EMS) guidelines and Business Energy Scotland’s recommendations.
5. **Stakeholder Engagement:** Empower employees to contribute to environmental initiatives through training and awareness programs, as highlighted in “Release the Collective Power of Your Employees for the Good of the Planet.”
6. **Innovation:** Explore innovative solutions for environmental challenges, such as the “Lighting Streets from Space for Less” initiative and Lumos Controls for Net Zero targets.

## Responsibilities

- **Management Functions:** The Sustainability team, led by the Owner, Ryan Laird, is responsible for overseeing the implementation and integration of this policy.
- **Employees:** All employees are expected to adhere to the principles outlined in this policy and actively participate in sustainability initiatives.
- **Verification:** Regular audits and assessments will be conducted to ensure compliance and continuous improvement of the EMS.

## Implementation Strategies

1. **Environmental Management Systems (EMS):** Adopt a flexible, phased approach to implementing EMS, as per the guidelines referenced in the document.
2. **Employee Training:** Deliver targeted training programs to align employee actions with environmental objectives.
3. **Policy Alignment:** Ensure alignment with related documents, such as the Energy Policy and the Sustainability Policy, to maintain a cohesive approach.
4. **Monitoring and Evaluation:** Measure progress against environmental benchmarks, leveraging tools such as the SBA ESG Self-Assessment.

## Review and Updates

This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to ensure its relevance and effectiveness. The latest version of the policy, Version 1.1, is currently in review and subject to updates based on feedback and organizational priorities.

- Reporting environmental concerns or incidents

We maintain transparent communication about our environmental performance through:

- Regular sustainability reports
- Stakeholder engagement
- Internal communication channels
- Public environmental disclosures

# Environmental Risk Policy

Green Orbit Digital is committed to identifying, assessing, and managing environmental risks across all aspects of our operations to protect the environment and ensure sustainable business practices.

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy establishes the framework for managing environmental risks within Green Orbit Digital's operations, ensuring compliance with ISO 14001 standards and alignment with our Environmental Management System (EMS).

## 2. Risk Assessment and Management

- Regular Environmental Risk Assessments covering energy use, resource consumption, waste production, and emissions
- Implementation of proactive measures to mitigate negative environmental impacts
- Monitoring and assessment of environmental conditions to track changes and identify potential risks

## 3. Key Risk Areas

### 3.1 Digital Operations

As a digital-focused company, we recognize and manage risks associated with:

- Energy consumption from digital operations and data centers
- Digital carbon footprint from cloud services and hosting
- E-waste management and responsible disposal of electronic equipment

## 4. Mitigation Strategies

- Implementation of energy-efficient cloud services and data centers
- Optimization of website and digital platform performance
- Use of green hosting providers powered by renewable energy
- Regular sustainability audits to evaluate environmental impact

## 5. Compliance and Reporting

Green Orbit Digital will:

- Comply with all relevant environmental legislation and regulations
- Conduct regular reviews of applicable laws and industry standards
- Maintain transparent reporting of environmental performance
- Engage with stakeholders to address environmental concerns

## 6. Responsibilities

All employees must:

- Comply with environmental policies and procedures
- Report environmental risks and incidents promptly
- Participate in environmental training and awareness programs

## 7. Review and Update

This policy will be reviewed annually or when significant changes occur in our operations or regulatory requirements. Updates will be made to ensure continuous improvement in our environmental risk management practices.
