## 1. Purpose and Scope

Impact Orbit Creative Group, including Green Orbit Digital, is committed to **environmental stewardship, sustainable practices, and the protection of biodiversity** across all operations.  

This policy establishes a structured framework for:

- Implementing and maintaining an **Environmental Management System (EMS)** aligned with ISO 14001 standards  
- Managing environmental risks across our operations  
- Minimizing environmental impact while promoting sustainability, compliance, and continual improvement  

It applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, suppliers, and stakeholders involved in activities under our control, including office operations, digital services, supply chain management, and business travel.

---

## 2. Policy Statement

We commit to:

- Minimizing environmental impacts across all operations  
- Promoting sustainable resource use and biodiversity conservation  
- Complying with all applicable environmental legislation, regulations, and standards  
- Continuously improving environmental performance  
- Engaging employees, suppliers, and stakeholders in environmental responsibility  

This policy supports broader sustainability initiatives, including SDGs 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation), 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy), and 15 (Life on Land).

---

## 3. Environmental Objectives

We aim to:

1. **Reduce Energy Consumption and Emissions**  
   - Transition to renewable energy sources where feasible  
   - Implement energy-efficient practices in offices and digital operations  
   - Monitor and reduce greenhouse gas emissions

2. **Minimize Waste and Promote Circular Economy**  
   - Reduce, reuse, and recycle materials  
   - Manage e-waste responsibly and comply with WEEE directives  
   - Optimize resource efficiency in procurement and operations

3. **Sustainable Water Use**  
   - Improve water efficiency in offices and operational sites  
   - Implement water-saving practices across facilities  

4. **Biodiversity and Ecosystem Protection**  
   - Avoid operations in sensitive ecological areas  
   - Support initiatives that restore and protect habitats  

5. **Employee and Stakeholder Engagement**  
   - Deliver training programs and awareness campaigns  
   - Encourage participation in sustainability initiatives  
   - Foster a culture of environmental responsibility

---

## 4. Environmental Risk Management

### 4.1 Purpose

Environmental risk management ensures that all potential negative impacts from our operations are identified, assessed, mitigated, and monitored. This aligns with our EMS and ISO 14001 requirements.

### 4.2 Key Risk Areas

- **Digital Operations**: Energy use, carbon footprint, e-waste, and cloud services  
- **Facilities Management**: Energy, water, and waste management  
- **Procurement and Supply Chain**: Supplier sustainability and material sourcing  
- **Business Travel**: Emissions from commuting and transport  

### 4.3 Mitigation Strategies

- Use **energy-efficient equipment** and renewable-powered hosting services  
- Optimize digital platforms and campaign delivery to reduce energy consumption  
- Conduct **regular sustainability audits**  
- Engage suppliers on sustainable practices and emissions reduction  
- Implement proactive measures for water, energy, and waste management  

---

## 5. Implementation

Environmental management is achieved through:

- **Environmental Management System (EMS)**: ISO 14001-aligned processes for planning, monitoring, and improving performance  
- **Risk Assessments**: Regular evaluation of environmental risks and mitigation strategies  
- **Monitoring and Reporting**: Track environmental KPIs, emissions, energy usage, and waste  
- **Employee Training and Engagement**: Programs to build awareness, skills, and accountability  
- **Policy Integration**: Alignment with related policies, including Energy Management, Emissions Reduction, Sustainability, and Biodiversity

| Role | Responsibility |

## 7. Communication and Engagement

- Share **environmental performance** through sustainability reports and internal dashboards  
- Maintain **transparent reporting** to stakeholders  
- Include environmental responsibility in **employee onboarding and induction**  
- Foster participation in company-led and community environmental programs  

---

## 8. Compliance and Legal Obligations

- Comply with UK and international environmental regulations, including ISO 14001 standards  
- Monitor regulatory updates and align policies accordingly  
- Maintain records and evidence of compliance for internal and external audits  

---

## 9. Review and Continuous Improvement

- The policy is **reviewed annually** or when significant operational or regulatory changes occur  
- Updates are made to reflect **best practices, technological advancements, and organizational priorities**  
- Continuous improvement is embedded in the EMS through **regular audits, employee feedback, and performance monitoring**
