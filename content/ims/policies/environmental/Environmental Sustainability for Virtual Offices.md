## 1. Introduction

At **Impact Orbit**, we are committed to environmental sustainability and recognise the importance of reducing our ecological footprint. This policy provides guidance for employees working in virtual offices to prioritise environmentally preferred products, practices, and behaviours, including recycling, energy efficiency, and waste reduction.

---

## 2. Environmentally Preferred Products

### a. Energy-Efficient Equipment

- Employees are encouraged to use energy-efficient devices such as laptops, monitors, and printers that meet ENERGY STAR or equivalent standards.  
- Guidance will be provided on selecting devices and peripherals that minimise electricity consumption.

### b. Sustainable Office Supplies

- Use recycled or environmentally certified office supplies, including paper, pens, and folders.  
- Minimise paper usage wherever possible and prioritise digital alternatives.

### c. Green Cleaning Products

- Adopt environmentally friendly cleaning products that are biodegradable, non-toxic, and free from harmful chemicals.  
- Resources and recommendations will be provided for selecting sustainable cleaning products for home or virtual office spaces.

---

## 3. Waste Reduction and Recycling

### a. Paperless Practices

- Promote paperless practices by using digital documents, electronic signatures, and online collaboration tools.  
- Encourage double-sided printing only when printing is necessary.

### b. Recycling Programme

- Establish a virtual office recycling programme for proper disposal of paper, cardboard, plastics, and electronics.  
- Provide guidance on sorting and recycling in accordance with local regulations.

### c. E-Waste Management

- Educate employees on the proper disposal of electronic devices.  
- Encourage donation, refurbishment, or recycling of old electronics to avoid landfill disposal.

---

## 4. Energy Conservation

### a. Power Management

- Employees should power down devices when not in use and enable power-saving features such as sleep mode.  
- Guidance will be provided for optimising computer and monitor energy settings.

### b. Virtual Meetings

- Encourage virtual meetings and teleconferencing to reduce travel and associated carbon emissions.  
- Training on efficient virtual meeting practices will be provided.

---

## 5. Employee Engagement and Education

### a. Training and Awareness

- Offer training and resources to raise awareness of environmental issues and sustainable practices.  
- Share updates on environmental initiatives and achievements within the organisation.

### b. Employee Feedback

- Employees are encouraged to suggest improvements and share ideas on sustainability practices.  
- Establish channels for sharing best practices and success stories.

---

## 6. Compliance and Monitoring

### a. Policy Compliance

- Employees are expected to understand and comply with this policy.  
- Non-compliance will be addressed through education and corrective measures.

### b. Performance Tracking

- Track environmental performance metrics such as energy consumption, waste reduction, and recycling rates.  
- Regularly review progress towards sustainability goals and objectives.

---

## 7. Conclusion

By following this policy, **Impact Orbit** demonstrates its commitment to reducing environmental impact and promoting sustainable practices in virtual office operations. Through collective effort and ongoing engagement, we aim to create a greener, more environmentally responsible workplace for the benefit of current and future generations.
