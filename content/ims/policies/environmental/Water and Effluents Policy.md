## Introduction

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, we recognize water as a critical resource essential to both the environment and our operations. We are committed to its responsible use, conservation, and management. This policy outlines our approach to minimising water consumption, preventing pollution, and managing effluents sustainably and responsibly.

## Scope

This policy applies to all operations, facilities, and activities of **Impact Orbit Creative Group** and its subsidiaries that involve water usage, wastewater, or effluent management.

## Our Commitment

1. **Water Conservation and Efficiency**  
   We aim to reduce water consumption and enhance efficiency by:
   - Implementing water-saving technologies and best practices  
   - Monitoring and managing water use regularly  
   - Educating employees on effective water conservation methods  

2. **Water Quality and Pollution Prevention**  
   We strive to protect water quality and prevent contamination by:
   - Ensuring effluents and discharges meet or exceed regulatory standards  
   - Implementing measures to reduce pollution of water sources  
   - Conducting regular water quality testing and monitoring  

3. **Effluent Management**  
   We manage effluents responsibly to minimise environmental impact:  
   - Treating wastewater before discharge  
   - Exploring recycling and reuse opportunities for water  
   - Reducing effluent volume and toxicity wherever possible  

4. **Stakeholder Engagement and Compliance**  
   We engage stakeholders and ensure compliance with all relevant regulations:  
   - Collaborating with local communities, regulators, and environmental organizations  
   - Adhering to local, national, and international water and effluent standards  
   - Maintaining transparency on water management and effluent practices  

5. **Risk Management and Emergency Preparedness**  
   We proactively manage risks related to water and effluents by:  
   - Identifying potential risks and assessing their impacts  
   - Implementing emergency response plans for water-related incidents  

6. **Continuous Improvement**  
   We are committed to ongoing improvement in water and effluent management by:  
   - Regularly reviewing and updating policies and procedures  
   - Keeping up-to-date with advances in water conservation and effluent treatment technologies  
   - Setting measurable targets for water reduction and effluent management  

## Responsibilities

- **Management**: Ensures implementation, enforcement, and resourcing of this policy  
- **Environmental Management Department**: Oversees water and effluent initiatives, compliance, and monitoring  
- **All Employees**: Expected to adhere to the policy and support water conservation and effluent management goals  

## Review and Update

- This policy will be reviewed at least annually to ensure continued effectiveness and relevance  
- Updates will reflect changes in technology, regulations, and business operations  

## Approval

This policy has been approved by the executive management of **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, demonstrating our commitment to responsible water and effluent management.
