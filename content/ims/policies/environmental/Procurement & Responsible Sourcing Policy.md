## Introduction

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, we are committed to responsible sourcing practices that respect human rights, promote environmental stewardship, and foster positive social impact across our supply chain. This policy establishes a clear framework for procurement decisions, ensuring alignment with ethical standards, sustainable practices, and ISO 14001 Environmental Management System requirements.

### Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide strategic guidance for procuring goods and services, ensuring that all sourcing activities:

- Promote sustainability and environmental responsibility
- Uphold the highest ethical and social standards
- Align with ISO 14001 requirements
- Maintain transparency, accountability, and fairness in procurement

### Scope

This policy applies to:

- All procurement activities conducted within Impact Orbit Creative Group
- Suppliers, contractors, and service providers engaged in our operations
- All stages of the sourcing process, including supplier selection, onboarding, and performance monitoring

---

## Policy Statement

Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to ethical, sustainable, and efficient procurement practices. Our procurement decisions consider environmental, social, and economic impacts alongside cost and quality, fostering positive outcomes for people, planet, and business.

---

## Key Principles

### 1. Environmental Responsibility

- Source materials and services with minimal environmental impact
- Prioritize suppliers with recognized environmental certifications (e.g., ISO 14001, EU Ecolabel)
- Encourage waste reduction, energy efficiency, and sustainable resource use
- Implement ongoing measurement of environmental impacts across the supply chain

### 2. Social and Ethical Standards

- Ensure fair labor practices, safe working conditions, and respect for human rights
- Prohibit child labor, forced labor, modern slavery, and human trafficking
- Promote diversity and inclusion in supplier selection
- Uphold transparency, honesty, and ethical conduct in all procurement activities

### 3. Supplier Selection Criteria

Suppliers and service providers will be evaluated based on:

- Commitment to sustainability and ethical practices
- Quality, reliability, and compliance with legal requirements
- Financial stability and risk management capabilities
- Alignment with Impact Orbit Creative Group’s ESG values and strategic objectives

### 4. Monitoring and Evaluation

- Conduct regular supplier audits and performance assessments
- Track sustainability and ethical compliance metrics
- Document and address any non-compliance issues promptly
- Continuously review and improve procurement procedures

---

## Objectives

1. **Sustainability**: Reduce environmental impacts in line with ISO 14001 standards and organizational goals.
2. **Ethics**: Maintain fairness, transparency, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
3. **Efficiency**: Streamline procurement processes for cost-effectiveness without compromising quality.
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Regularly enhance procurement practices based on feedback, audits, and evolving best practices.
