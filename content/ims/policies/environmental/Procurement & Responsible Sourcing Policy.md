## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide a clear and strategic direction for the procurement of goods and services, ensuring alignment with ethical standards and sustainable practices while complying with ISO 14001: Environmental Management System standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all procurement activities conducted within the organization and is intended to guide decision-making processes to ensure responsible and transparent sourcing of goods and services.

## Policy Statement

Our organization is committed to ethical procurement practices that promote sustainability, fairness, and efficiency. This policy aligns with the principles outlined in ISO 14001 and supports our organizational goals for environmental responsibility and sustainable business practices.

## Objectives

1. **Sustainability**: Prioritize suppliers and goods that adhere to sustainable practices, reducing environmental impact in line with ISO 14001 standards.
2. **Ethics**: Maintain the highest ethical standards in procurement, ensuring fairness, transparency, and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.
3. **Efficiency**: Streamline procurement processes to achieve cost-effectiveness while maintaining quality and reliability.
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Regularly review and enhance procurement procedures to adapt to evolving business needs and environmental standards.

# Policy Statement

Green Orbit Digital is committed to responsible sourcing practices that promote sustainability, ethical conduct, and positive social impact throughout our supply chain. This policy establishes the framework for ensuring our procurement decisions align with our environmental, social, and economic values.

## Scope

This policy applies to all procurement activities, suppliers, contractors, and service providers engaged by Green Orbit Digital. It covers all stages of the sourcing process, from supplier selection to performance monitoring.

## Key Principles

### 1. Environmental Responsibility

- Source materials and services with minimal environmental impact
- Prioritize suppliers with environmental certifications (e.g., ISO 14001, EU Ecolabel)
- Promote waste reduction and resource efficiency in the supply chain

### 2. Social and Ethical Standards

- Ensure fair labor practices and safe working conditions
- Support diversity and inclusion in supplier selection
- Prohibit modern slavery and human trafficking

### 3. Supplier Selection Criteria

- Sustainability commitment and environmental performance
- Quality and reliability standards
- Financial stability and risk management capabilities
- Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

### 4. Monitoring and Evaluation

- Regular supplier audits and performance reviews
- Tracking of sustainability metrics and improvements
- Documentation of compliance with policy requirements

## Implementation

All procurement decisions must consider environmental and social impacts alongside traditional factors such as cost and quality. Suppliers are required to demonstrate their commitment to sustainability and ethical practices through appropriate documentation and ongoing performance measurement.

## Compliance and Reporting

Non-compliance with this policy may result in termination of supplier relationships. Regular reports on responsible sourcing performance will be provided to senior management.

# **Introduction**

At [Your Business Name], we are committed to responsible sourcing practices that respect human rights, the environment, and the communities where we operate. This policy outlines our approach to ensuring that our supply chain reflects our values and ethical standards.

**Scope**

This policy applies to all our suppliers, contractors, and business partners involved in the supply chain.

**Our Commitment**

1. **Ethical Practices**: We are committed to conducting our business ethically and expect the same from our suppliers. This includes:

- Ensuring transparency and honesty in all business dealings.
- Rejecting bribery, corruption, and unethical practices.

2. **Human Rights and Labor Standards**: We expect our suppliers to uphold human rights and adhere to international labor standards, including:

- Prohibiting child labor and forced labor.
- Providing safe and healthy working conditions.
- Ensuring fair wages and benefits.
- Respecting the rights of workers to associate freely.

3. **Environmental Stewardship:** We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and expect our suppliers to share this commitment by:

- Implementing sustainable and eco-friendly practices.
- Reducing waste, emissions, and resource consumption.
- Managing environmental risks effectively.

4. **Quality and Safety:** We prioritize high standards of quality and safety in our products and services and require our suppliers to meet these standards by:

- Adhering to all applicable quality and safety regulations.
- Conducting regular quality checks and audits.
- Ensuring products are safe for consumers and the environment.

5. **Supplier Diversity and Inclusion:** We value diversity and are committed to inclusivity in our supply chain by:

- Encouraging the participation of diverse suppliers, including minority-owned, women-owned, and small businesses.
- Promoting equal opportunities in our procurement processes.

6. **Monitoring and Compliance:** We will actively monitor our supply chain for compliance with this policy by:

- Conducting regular assessments and audits of suppliers.
- Requiring suppliers to provide evidence of compliance.
- Addressing any violations promptly and effectively.

**Responsibilities**

- Our Procurement Department is responsible for implementing this policy and ensuring compliance.
- All employees involved in procurement and supply chain management are expected to adhere to this policy.
- Suppliers are required to commit to this policy as a condition of doing business with us.

**Training and Awareness**

- We will provide necessary training and resources to our employees to effectively implement this policy.
- We will raise awareness among our suppliers about our responsible sourcing expectations.

**Continuous Improvement**

- We are committed to continuously improving our sourcing practices.
- We will regularly review and update this policy to reflect evolving standards and best practices.
