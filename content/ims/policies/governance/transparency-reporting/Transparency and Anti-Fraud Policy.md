## 1. Introduction
At Impact Orbit Creative Group, we uphold the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and ethical conduct in all business dealings. This policy outlines our commitment to preventing fraud, bribery, and inappropriate gift-giving and establishes guidelines and procedures for maintaining transparency and compliance with applicable laws and regulations.  

The Company has a zero-tolerance policy toward bribery and corruption and is committed to acting fairly and with integrity in all business dealings worldwide.

---

## 2. Purpose and Scope
This policy aims to:  

- Ensure compliance with anti-bribery laws, rules, and regulations globally.  
- Enable employees and associated persons to understand risks and recognise, prevent, and report wrongdoing.  
- Provide secure reporting channels and ensure reported information is addressed effectively.  
- Maintain a robust framework for managing suspected bribery or unethical conduct.  

**Applicability:**  
- All employees (permanent and temporary).  
- Associated persons, including contractors, consultants, agents, suppliers, and sponsors.  

---

## 3. Legal Obligations
The policy is based on the **Bribery Act 2010** and applies to operations in the UK and abroad.  

**Prohibited actions include:**  
- Offering, promising, or giving a bribe.  
- Requesting, agreeing to receive, or accepting a bribe.  
- Bribing public officials.  

**Facilitation payments** are strictly prohibited. Employees can be held personally liable for violations, and the Company may face fines and reputational damage.

---

## 4. Fraud Prevention
The Company is committed to preventing fraud, including financial fraud, misrepresentation, embezzlement, and other deceptive practices. Employees must:  

- Act with honesty and integrity.  
- Safeguard company assets and confidential information.  
- Report suspected fraud promptly to management or the Compliance Officer.  

---

## 5. Bribery Prevention
Bribery in any form, direct or indirect, is strictly prohibited. Employees must:  

- Refrain from offering, giving, or accepting bribes or kickbacks.  
- Conduct business fairly, transparently, and ethically.  
- Report suspected bribery immediately to management or the Compliance Officer.  

**Note:** Industry practices may vary, but strict adherence to this policy is required.

---

## 6. Gifts and Entertainment
Exchanging gifts and hospitality is permissible only when it does not compromise integrity. Employees must:  

- Exercise discretion and good judgment when giving or receiving gifts or hospitality.  
- Avoid gifts or entertainment that may influence business decisions or create conflicts of interest.  
- Seek approval for gifts or entertainment exceeding nominal value or raising potential concerns.  

**Gifts to clients or partners must:**  

- Not intend to secure or reward a business advantage.  
- Comply with local laws.  
- Be given in the Companyâ€™s name.  
- Exclude cash or cash equivalents.  
- Be appropriate in value, timing, and transparency.  
- Be approved in advance by a Director.

---

## 7. Responsibilities and Reporting
All employees and associated persons are responsible for:  

- Complying with this policy and anti-bribery legislation.  
- Preventing, detecting, and reporting fraud, bribery, or unethical conduct.  
- Reporting suspected violations promptly via the **Public Interest Disclosure Policy** or **Whistleblowing Policy**.  

The Company protects whistleblowers and prohibits retaliation for good-faith reporting. Contracts with third parties must include clauses ensuring adherence to this policy.

---

## 8. Record-Keeping
All accounts, receipts, invoices, and related documents must be accurate and complete. Off-the-record accounts are strictly prohibited.

---

## 9. Sanctions for Breach
Violations may result in:  

- Disciplinary action up to and including termination.  
- Legal action for individuals engaging in fraudulent, corrupt, or unethical conduct.  
- Suspension or termination of contracts with associated persons.

---

## 10. Training and Awareness
The Company provides:  

- Regular training on fraud, bribery, gifts, and hospitality risks.  
- Guidance on red flags, reporting procedures, and legal/ethical implications.  
- Communication of the zero-tolerance approach to all business partners.

---

## 11. Compliance Oversight
The **Compliance Officer / Anti-Corruption Officer** is responsible for:  

- Ensuring adherence to this policy.  
- Conducting periodic audits and reviews.  
- Reporting compliance status to the Directors.  

---

## 12. Examples of Potential Risks
Situations that may raise bribery concerns include:  

- Requests for unexpected commissions or fees.  
- Payment requests without invoices or proper documentation.  
- Lavish or extraordinary gifts or hospitality from third parties.  
- Non-standard or excessive third-party invoices.  

**Goal:** Effectively manage risks related to bribery, fraud, extortion, and money laundering.  

**Checklist:**  

- Annual conflict of interest questionnaires for Board members and officers.  
- Continuous monitoring and reporting of risks related to fraud and corruption.

---

## 13. Conclusion
By adhering to this Anti-Fraud, Bribery, and Gifts Policy, Impact Orbit Creative Group reaffirms its commitment to ethical business practices, integrity, and compliance with laws and regulations. We strive to maintain a culture of transparency, accountability, and trust within our organization and in all interactions with clients, partners, and stakeholders.
