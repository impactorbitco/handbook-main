# Ethical Policy

## Introduction

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, we are committed to upholding the highest ethical standards across all operations. As a responsible leader in the space and digital communications sector, we recognise our duty to conduct business ethically, support sustainability, and create positive social impact. This policy sets out our values, ethical commitments, and operational principles.

---

## Core Values

Our core values guide every decision and interaction:

1. **Integrity** – Acting honestly, fairly, and ethically at all times.  
2. **Transparency** – Maintaining openness and clarity in communications and practices.  
3. **Respect** – Valuing diversity, treating others with dignity, and fostering an inclusive culture.  
4. **Excellence** – Striving for high-quality solutions and services in every endeavor.  
5. **Responsibility** – Considering the social, environmental, and economic impact of our actions.

---

## Code of Ethics

1. **Legality** – Comply with all relevant laws, regulations, and industry standards.  
2. **Loyalty** – Prioritise the interests of clients, partners, and employees while upholding ethical obligations.  
3. **Transparency** – Provide accurate, clear information to foster trust with stakeholders.  
4. **Impartiality** – Treat all individuals and organisations fairly, without bias or favoritism.  
5. **Professionalism** – Maintain diligence, competence, and continuous improvement in all activities.  
6. **Value of the Individual** – Respect the intrinsic worth of every person and promote equality.  
7. **Quality** – Deliver products and services to the highest standards.  
8. **Anti-Money Laundering** – Actively prevent financial crimes and cooperate with authorities.  
9. **Confidentiality** – Protect proprietary and sensitive information entrusted to us.  
10. **Data Protection** – Handle sensitive data responsibly and comply with data protection laws.  
11. **Intellectual Property** – Respect and safeguard the IP rights of others and our own creations.  
12. **Environmental Responsibility** – Minimise ecological impact and promote sustainability.  
13. **Security** – Implement measures to protect data and digital assets from threats.  
14. **Sustainable Development** – Support initiatives that balance social, environmental, and economic sustainability.

---

## Commitments

- **Integrity and Professionalism** – Apply ethical standards in all client and partner interactions.  
- **Confidentiality** – Protect client and employee information.  
- **Honesty and Accuracy** – Provide reliable, truthful advice and reporting.  
- **Exceeding Expectations** – Deliver high-quality work on time and within scope.  
- **Continuous Learning** – Stay informed on industry best practices and evolving standards.  
- **Intellectual Property Respect** – Properly attribute and respect creative works.  
- **Inclusive Workplace** – Foster a safe, respectful, and equitable work environment.  
- **Conflict of Interest Management** – Identify and disclose any potential conflicts.  
- **Reputation Protection** – Uphold the company’s and industry’s integrity.

---

## Ethical Trading and Modern Slavery

Impact Orbit Creative Group adheres to the **Ethical Trading Initiative Base Code** and the **Modern Slavery Act 2015**, ensuring suppliers maintain ethical standards and actively prevent human trafficking or forced labor.

---

## Environmental and Sustainability Commitments

### Clean Creatives Pledge
- Decline contracts with fossil fuel companies.  
- Promote sustainability through all marketing and business practices.  

### The Ethical Move Pledge
- Advocate for transparent and responsible marketing practices.  
- Avoid deceptive tactics and prioritise ethics in all campaigns.

---

## Governance and Compliance

- **Training** – All employees complete annual ethics training.  
- **Reporting** – Ethical concerns can be reported anonymously to the Ethics Committee.  
- **Auditing** – Conduct regular internal audits of operations and supplier practices.  
- **Continuous Improvement** – Policies are reviewed annually and updated to reflect legal, social, and environmental changes.

## UN Sustainable Development Goals Alignment

Impact Orbit’s ethical practices support:

- **SDG 8**: Decent Work and Economic Growth – Fair employment practices.  
- **SDG 12**: Responsible Consumption and Production – Sustainable sourcing and waste reduction.  
- **SDG 13**: Climate Action – Environmental commitments and Clean Creatives pledge.  
- **SDG 16**: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions – Transparency, anti-corruption, and ethical governance.
