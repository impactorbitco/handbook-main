# Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Policy

## Purpose

This Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Policy outlines our organization's commitment to sustainability, ethical practices, and social responsibility. As a Level 1 â€“ Strategic Direction document, it serves as a framework for aligning our actions with our values, ensuring long-term positive impacts on society and the environment.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, stakeholders, and partners of the organization, guiding our approach to sustainability, ethical governance, and community engagement.

## Objectives

- To promote environmental sustainability through initiatives such as the Youth Environmental Programme and Bright Green Future.
- To encourage goodwill activities that enhance the well-being of communities and stakeholders.
- To align our practices with global standards, including the ISO 14001 Environmental Management System and ISO EMS A.5.1 Policies for Information Security.
- To support NGOs by understanding CSR trends and identifying opportunities for collaborative impact.

## Responsibilities

The management function overseeing this policy is **Sustainability**, with **Ryan Laird** as the document owner. Key responsibilities include:

- Ensuring compliance with ISO 14001 standards.
- Regularly reviewing and updating the policy as necessary.
- Collaborating with stakeholders to implement CSR initiatives effectively.

## Key Areas of Focus

1. **Environmental Sustainability**
    - Adopting environmentally responsible practices across operations.
    - Supporting programs like the Youth Environmental Programme and Bright Green Future to foster ecological awareness.
2. **Social Responsibility**
    - Engaging in community goodwill activities that promote social equity and improve quality of life.
    - Partnering with NGOs to address pressing social challenges.
3. **Ethical Governance**
    - Maintaining transparency and accountability in all CSR-related activities.
    - Adhering to standards outlined in related documents such as the Sustainability Policy and Guidance on Social Responsibility.

## Implementation

- The policy is **in progress** and currently **in review**, with **low priority** for immediate action.
- The document's status will be updated upon final approval, and its implementation will be overseen by the Sustainability function.
