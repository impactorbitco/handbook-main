## Introduction

Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to ensuring that all business operations comply with applicable laws, regulations, and internal policies. This policy outlines our approach to identifying, managing, and adhering to our legal and regulatory obligations across all operational areas.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to:

- Provide a structured approach to compliance management.
- Ensure that all employees, contractors, and business partners understand their legal responsibilities.
- Minimise legal, financial, and reputational risks associated with non-compliance.
- Promote a culture of accountability and ethical business conduct.

## Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees, contractors, and third-party partners.
- All operations, departments, and functions within the organization.
- Activities related to regulatory reporting, contractual obligations, corporate governance, and compliance monitoring.

## Key Principles

1. **Legal Compliance**  
   - Adhere to all applicable local, national, and international laws, regulations, and standards.  
   - Maintain up-to-date knowledge of relevant regulatory changes affecting our business.

2. **Internal Standards and Policies**  
   - Align organizational practices with internal policies, IMS requirements, and industry best practices.  
   - Ensure policies are clearly communicated and accessible to all employees and partners.

3. **Risk Identification and Mitigation**  
   - Conduct regular compliance risk assessments.  
   - Implement controls to prevent, detect, and respond to potential non-compliance issues.

4. **Training and Awareness**  
   - Provide training for employees on legal obligations relevant to their roles.  
   - Promote awareness of ethical business practices and compliance responsibilities.

5. **Monitoring and Reporting**  
   - Monitor operations to ensure compliance with legal and policy requirements.  
   - Establish reporting channels for potential breaches or concerns, including anonymous reporting mechanisms.  

6. **Corrective Action and Continuous Improvement**  
   - Investigate all reported compliance issues promptly and implement corrective actions.  
   - Review policies and procedures regularly to improve compliance and reduce risk.

## Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for overall implementation, oversight, and approval of this policy.  
- **Management**: Ensure employees and contractors comply with legal obligations and internal policies.  
- **Compliance Department/Officer**: Monitor adherence, conduct audits, provide guidance, and report on compliance status.  
- **All Employees and Partners**: Understand and follow this policy, participate in training, and report potential compliance breaches.

## Review and Update

- This policy will be reviewed annually or as required by changes in legislation, business operations, or IMS requirements.  
- Updates will be communicated to all employees and relevant stakeholders promptly.
