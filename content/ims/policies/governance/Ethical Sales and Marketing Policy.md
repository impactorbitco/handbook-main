Impact Orbit is committed to maintaining the highest ethical standards in all sales, marketing, and customer engagement activities. This policy ensures that our operations are transparent, responsible, and aligned with social, environmental, and governance principles.

---

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy applies to all sales, marketing, communications, product information, advertising, and data handling activities conducted by Impact Orbit. It provides guidance for:

- Truthful and responsible marketing practices.
- Transparent and accurate product information.
- Ethical customer engagement.
- Protection of customer privacy.
- Alignment with sustainability and social responsibility objectives.

---

## 2. Core Principles

### 2.1 Truthfulness and Transparency
- Marketing and sales communications must be honest, clear, and non-deceptive.
- Claims about products or services must be substantiated with verifiable evidence.
- Advertising and promotional materials must not mislead customers or exaggerate benefits.

### 2.2 Customer Privacy and Data Protection
- Collect, store, and process customer data in compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations.
- Obtain explicit consent for all data collection and use.
- Implement robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access or breaches.

### 2.3 Inclusivity and Cultural Sensitivity
- Ensure all marketing campaigns and sales approaches are inclusive and respectful of diversity.
- Avoid content or messaging that perpetuates stereotypes or discriminatory practices.
- Consider cultural sensitivities in global and cross-sector communications.

### 2.4 Ethical Sales Practices
- Engage with customers professionally and honestly.
- Avoid high-pressure, manipulative, or exploitative sales tactics.
- Ensure sales approaches comply with legal, ethical, and industry standards.

### 2.5 Environmental Responsibility
- Prioritize sustainable marketing practices, such as digital-first campaigns, low-carbon initiatives, and eco-conscious content production.
- Avoid greenwashing or making unsupported environmental claims.
- Evaluate the environmental impact of marketing campaigns and implement mitigation measures where possible.

### 2.6 Compliance and Legal Adherence
- Follow all relevant local, national, and international laws regarding marketing, sales, advertising, and customer engagement.
- Regularly review policies and practices to ensure continued compliance.

---

## 3. Sales and Marketing Operations

### 3.1 Lead Generation and Market Segmentation
- Identify target audiences with a focus on sustainability-driven organizations and stakeholders.
- Segment markets by business type, sustainability goals, and growth stage.
- Employ ethical lead generation practices without spamming or deceptive outreach.

### 3.2 Proposal and Client Screening
- Evaluate potential clients and projects for alignment with Impact Orbit’s ethical, social, and environmental values.
- Reject engagements with entities linked to harmful industries, unethical practices, or misaligned social/environmental stances.
- Ensure political neutrality and avoid associations with authoritarian regimes.

### 3.3 Campaign Execution
- Ensure marketing campaigns are evidence-based, truthful, and socially responsible.
- Incorporate accessibility, inclusivity, and cultural considerations in all campaigns.
- Monitor environmental impact and implement mitigation where necessary.

### 3.4 Customer Feedback and Grievances
- Maintain accessible channels for customer feedback and complaints.
- Respond promptly and transparently to issues raised by customers or stakeholders.
- Review feedback regularly to improve services, products, and communications.

---

## 4. Implementation and Monitoring

- **Leadership Oversight:** Management ensures all sales and marketing activities comply with this policy.
- **Training:** Employees and contractors undergo regular training on ethical sales, marketing practices, and compliance.
- **Auditing:** Conduct periodic audits of campaigns, sales processes, and data handling practices.
- **Continuous Improvement:** Incorporate feedback and lessons learned to refine policies and procedures.

---

## 5. ESG Alignment

- Align marketing and sales activities with Impact Orbit’s ESG objectives.
- Report on progress toward sustainability, diversity, and social responsibility goals.
- Avoid greenwashing and maintain full transparency in environmental claims.

---

## 6. Approval and Accountability

This policy is approved by Impact Orbit’s leadership, reflecting the company’s commitment to ethical, responsible, and sustainable sales and marketing practices. Compliance with this policy is mandatory for all employees, partners, and contractors.
