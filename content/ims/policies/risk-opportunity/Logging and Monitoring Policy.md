title: "Logging and Monitoring Policy"

## Purpose

The purpose of this Logging and Monitoring Policy is to establish guidelines and procedures for logging and monitoring activities within the organization to ensure compliance with ISO/IEC 27001 standards. This policy aims to mitigate risks, enhance security, and support the organization's strategic direction by maintaining transparency and accountability in all information security management processes.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party service providers who have access to the organization’s information systems. It encompasses all IT infrastructure, including but not limited to servers, networks, applications, cloud services, and endpoints.

---

## Policy Statement

The organization recognizes the critical importance of logging and monitoring activities in maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets. The following principles will guide the implementation of this policy:

1. **Log Collection and Retention**
    - All relevant system and user activities must be logged and stored securely.
    - Logs must include, at a minimum, details of access, modifications, and system events.
    - Logs will be retained for a period that complies with legal, regulatory, and business requirements.
2. **Monitoring**
    - Monitoring tools will be implemented to detect unauthorized access, system anomalies, and potential security breaches.
    - Real-time alerts will be configured for critical systems to ensure timely responses to incidents.
3. **Access Control**
    - Access to logging and monitoring tools will be restricted to authorized personnel only.
    - Logs must be protected against unauthorized access, modification, or deletion.
4. **Compliance and Review**
    - Logging and monitoring practices must comply with ISO/IEC 27001 and other relevant standards.
    - Regular audits will be conducted to ensure adherence to this policy and to identify areas for improvement.
5. **Incident Response**
    - Logs will be used as a resource for investigating security incidents.
    - The organization will ensure timely and effective responses to incidents identified through monitoring activities.

---

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Policy Owner (Ryan Laird):** Responsible for maintaining and updating this policy.
- **IT Team:** Responsible for implementing logging and monitoring tools, ensuring compliance, and conducting regular audits.
- **All Employees and Contractors:** Responsible for adhering to the policy and reporting any suspicious activity to the IT team.
