## Purpose

The purpose of this document is to establish a clear and effective policy and strategy for asset management aligned with ISO 55001 standards. It aims to provide a strategic direction for managing assets within the organization to ensure their optimal value and alignment with organizational objectives.

## Scope

This policy applies to all assets within the organization that fall under the scope of ISO 55001: Asset Management System. It is applicable to all stakeholders, including employees, contractors, and any relevant third parties involved in the management or use of these assets.

## Policy Statement

We are committed to implementing and maintaining an asset management system that upholds the principles of efficiency, sustainability, and risk management. By adhering to ISO 55001 standards, we will ensure that all assets are managed effectively to support organizational goals, enhance performance, and deliver value to stakeholders.

## Objectives

1. **Alignment with Strategic Goals**: Ensure that asset management activities align with the organization’s strategic direction and objectives.
2. **Optimization of Asset Value**: Maximize the value derived from assets by balancing performance, cost, and risk.
3. **Compliance and Governance**: Comply with all relevant standards, including ISO 55001, and ensure robust governance of assets.
4. **Sustainability**: Promote the sustainable use and management of assets to support long-term organizational success.
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Foster a culture of continuous improvement in asset management practices.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for the ownership and oversight of this policy and strategy.
- **Last Edited By**: Ryan Laird, as of May 1, 2025, ensures the policy remains updated and aligned with organizational needs.
- **Stakeholders**: All employees, contractors, and relevant third parties are expected to adhere to this policy.

## Implementation

The implementation of this policy will involve the following steps:

1. **Development of Asset Management Plans**: Create detailed plans for managing assets in line with this policy and ISO 55001 requirements.
2. **Training and Awareness**: Provide training to all relevant stakeholders on asset management principles and practices.
3. **Monitoring and Review**: Regularly monitor asset performance and review management practices to ensure continuous improvement.
4. **Compliance Audits**: Conduct periodic audits to verify compliance with ISO 55001 and other relevant standards.
5. **Risk Management**: Identify and mitigate risks associated with asset management to safeguard organizational objectives.
