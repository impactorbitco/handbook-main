## 1. Introduction
At Impact Orbit Creative Group, we welcome links to our website that are consistent with the principles outlined in this policy. Our aim is to maintain clarity, transparency, and a professional online presence while enabling appropriate referrals to our content. By linking to our website, you agree to comply with these guidelines.

---

## 2. Guidelines for Linking to Our Website
To ensure accurate representation of our brand and content, all links to our website must adhere to the following principles:  

- **Accuracy and Transparency:** Links must not be misleading or misrepresent the content of our website.  
- **Descriptive Link Text:** Always use clear, descriptive anchor text that indicates the destination page.  
- **Current URLs:** Links should point to the latest URLs of our website as published; outdated links should be updated when possible.  
- **Branding Restrictions:** Our logo, trademarks, or any proprietary branding elements may only be used for linking with our express written consent.

## 3. Links from Our Website
- Our website may include hyperlinks to external, third-party websites for informational or convenience purposes.  
- Such links do not imply endorsement, sponsorship, or recommendation of the linked site.

---

## 4. Link Removal
- You agree to remove any link to our website upon request, within a reasonable timeframe.  
- Requests for removal of links from our website to your content will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Removal will be actioned at our discretion unless you have a legal entitlement to require it.  

---

## 5. Policy Amendments
- Impact Orbit Creative Group reserves the right to update or amend this Linking Policy at any time.  
- Updated policies will be published on our website and take effect immediately upon publication.  

---

## 7. Compliance and Best Practices
We encourage website operators to:  
- Ensure that linking respects copyright, intellectual property, and brand integrity.  
- Maintain links responsibly to prevent broken links or misrepresentation.  
- Monitor linked content regularly to ensure continued accuracy and relevance.  

---

This policy ensures that linking practices to and from the Impact Orbit Creative Group website are professional, transparent, and mutually respectful, promoting a positive online experience for users and protecting our brand and content.
