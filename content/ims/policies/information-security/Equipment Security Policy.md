# Equipment Security Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a framework for securing company equipment to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This policy aligns with the organization's commitment to maintaining information security as part of the ISO/IEC 27001 standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all company-owned or managed equipment, including but not limited to laptops, desktops, mobile devices, storage devices, and other technological assets that support business operations. All employees, contractors, and third-party users are required to adhere to this policy.

## Policy Statement

The organization is dedicated to ensuring the security of its equipment through proper management, handling, and usage protocols. All equipment must be safeguarded against unauthorized access, accidental loss, and theft to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

## Responsibilities

- **Employees**: Ensure proper handling and storage of equipment. Report any loss, theft, or damage immediately to the IT department.
- **IT Department**: Implement security measures such as encryption, access controls, and device tracking. Monitor compliance with the policy.
- **Management**: Oversee and ensure adherence to the policy by all staff and contractors.

## Security Measures

1. **Access Control**: Equipment must only be accessed by authorized personnel. Passwords and authentication mechanisms must be in place.
2. **Device Encryption**: All portable devices, such as laptops and mobile phones, must have encryption enabled.
3. **Physical Security**: Equipment must not be left unattended in unsecured locations. Lockable cabinets or secure storage areas must be used where necessary.
4. **Remote Access**: Remote access to company devices must be conducted through secure channels, such as VPNs.
5. **Disposal**: When equipment is no longer in use, it must be disposed of securely to prevent data breaches.

## Compliance

This policy is a part of the organization’s Information Security Management System (ISMS) and aligns with the ISO/IEC 27001 standards. Non-compliance may result in disciplinary actions in accordance with the company handbook.

## Review and Maintenance

This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to ensure its effectiveness and compliance with relevant standards. Updates will be documented and communicated to all staff.
