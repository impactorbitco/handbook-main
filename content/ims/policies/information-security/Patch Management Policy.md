# Patch Management Policy

## Overview

This document outlines the policy for managing and applying patches to systems and software in compliance with ISO/IEC 27001 standards for Information Security Management. It is classified as a strategic Level 1 policy and forms part of the organization's commitment to maintaining a secure and resilient IT infrastructure.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all systems and applications within the organization are up-to-date with the latest patches, minimizing vulnerabilities and protecting against potential threats. Effective patch management is a critical component of the organization's Information Security Management System (ISMS).

## Scope

This policy applies to all systems, software, and devices used within the organization. It includes operating systems, applications, and any third-party tools that require updates or patches to address security vulnerabilities. All employees, contractors, and third-party vendors are required to comply with this policy.

## Policy Details

### 1. Patch Management Process

- **Identification:** The IT team will monitor for available patches or updates from trusted vendors and sources.
- **Assessment:** Each identified patch will be assessed for relevance, urgency, and potential impact on existing systems.
- **Testing:** Before deployment, patches will be tested in a controlled environment to ensure compatibility and minimize disruptions.
- **Deployment:** Approved patches will be deployed to production systems in a timely manner based on their priority.
- **Verification:** Post-deployment, the IT team will verify that the patches have been successfully applied.

### 2. Roles and Responsibilities

- **Owner (Ryan Laird):** Responsible for overseeing the implementation of this policy and ensuring compliance with ISO standards.
- **IT Team:** Responsible for monitoring, testing, and deploying patches.
- **Employees:** Required to report potential vulnerabilities and ensure their devices are regularly updated.

### 3. Compliance with Standards

This policy aligns with the ISO/IEC 27001 framework and Annex A8 Control Group - Physical. It supports the organization's commitment to maintaining an effective ISMS.

### 4. Exceptions

Any exceptions to this policy must be documented and approved by the policy owner. Exceptions will only be granted in cases where applying a patch poses a greater risk than not applying it.

### 5. Review and Maintenance

This policy will be reviewed annually or as needed to accommodate changes in technology, threats, or organizational structure.
