## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines and requirements for secure remote access to the organization's systems, networks, and information resources. This policy aims to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information while supporting remote work and flexible work arrangements.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party users who require remote access to the organization's information systems. It covers all methods of remote access, including but not limited to VPN connections, remote desktop access, and cloud-based services.

## Policy Statement

The organization is committed to maintaining the security of its information assets and ensuring compliance with the ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management System standard. Remote access will be granted based on business needs and must adhere to the following requirements:

### 1. Authorization and Access Control

- Remote access will only be granted to authorized personnel with legitimate business needs.
- Access credentials, such as usernames and passwords, must be unique to each user and comply with the organization's password policy.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all remote access connections to enhance security.

### 2. Secure Communication

- All remote access sessions must use encrypted communication protocols (e.g., VPN, SSL/TLS) to protect data in transit.
- Users must ensure that their devices are updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

### 3. Device Security

- Only organization-approved devices may be used for remote access.
- Personal devices must be configured in accordance with the organization's security policies before being granted access.

### 4. Monitoring and Logging

- All remote access activities will be logged and monitored to detect unauthorized access or suspicious activities.
- Logs will be retained in compliance with the organization's data retention policies.

### 5. User Responsibilities

- Users are responsible for protecting their access credentials and ensuring they are not shared with others.
- Users must report any security incidents or suspected breaches immediately to the Security Management team.

### 6. Compliance

- Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract.
- This policy will be reviewed and updated regularly to ensure its effectiveness and alignment with current security standards.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for maintaining and updating this policy.
- **Management Functions**: The Security team oversees the implementation of this policy and ensures compliance with ISO/IEC 27001.
- **Users**: All users are responsible for adhering to this policy and safeguarding the organization's information assets.
