# Clear Desk and Screen Policy

## Purpose

The purpose of this Clear Desk and Screen Policy is to ensure the security of sensitive and confidential information within the organization by promoting good practices for managing and safeguarding physical and digital workspaces. This policy aligns with the ISO/IEC 27001 standards for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS) and supports the organization's strategic approach to information security.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who have access to the company's physical and digital environments. It covers all workspaces, including desks, meeting rooms, and electronic devices used to access company data.

## Objectives

- Maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
- Minimize the risk of unauthorized access to sensitive information.
- Promote a culture of responsibility and awareness regarding information security.

## Policy Requirements

1. **Clear Desk Practices**
    - At the end of each workday, employees must ensure that all sensitive documents, notes, and files are removed from desks and securely stored.
    - Documents containing sensitive or confidential information must be shredded or disposed of securely if they are no longer needed.
    - Personal items should be kept to a minimum to avoid clutter and reduce risks.
2. **Clear Screen Practices**
    - All computers and devices must be locked when unattended, even for short periods.
    - Employees must log out of systems and applications at the end of the workday.
    - Screens should be positioned to avoid unauthorized individuals from viewing sensitive information (e.g., using screen privacy filters).
3. **Physical Security**
    - Filing cabinets and storage units containing sensitive information must be locked when not in use.
    - Access to physical workspaces must be limited to authorized personnel only.
4. **Electronic Document Management**
    - Digital files must be stored in designated and secure locations such as company-approved cloud storage or internal servers.
    - Temporary files or downloads must be deleted after use to prevent exposure.
5. **Reporting and Compliance**
    - Employees are required to report any breaches of this policy or potential risks to their immediate supervisor or the security team.
    - The organization reserves the right to conduct audits to ensure compliance with this policy.

## Responsibilities

- **Employees and Contractors**: Adhere to the policy and ensure workspaces and devices are secure at all times.
- **Security Team**: Monitor compliance, provide training, and address incidents related to this policy.
- **Management**: Enforce the policy and support a culture of information security throughout the organization.

## Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy must be approved by the Security Team and documented to ensure transparency and accountability.
