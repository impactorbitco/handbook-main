## Introduction

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, we recognise the importance of fostering positive, constructive relationships with the local communities where we operate. This policy sets out our commitment to supporting community development, respecting local cultures and traditions, and conducting our business responsibly and sustainably.

## Scope

This policy applies to all operations, employees, contractors, and business activities that have an impact on local communities.

## Our Commitments

1. **Community Engagement and Dialogue**  
   - Maintain open, respectful communication channels with community members  
   - Involve local representatives in decisions that may affect their communities  
   - Provide regular updates on our activities and potential impacts  

2. **Support for Local Development**  
   - Contribute to local education, health, and infrastructure initiatives  
   - Encourage employee volunteerism and community service  
   - Partner with local organisations on projects that benefit the community  

3. **Respect for Local Culture and Traditions**  
   - Operate sensitively with regard to local norms and cultural practices  
   - Support and participate in cultural events where appropriate  
   - Ensure business activities do not harm cultural sites or traditions  

4. **Economic Inclusion and Opportunity**  
   - Hire locally wherever feasible and provide fair wages  
   - Source goods and services from local suppliers  
   - Offer training and development to enhance local skills and capacity  

5. **Environmental Stewardship**  
   - Implement sustainable practices to minimise our environmental impact  
   - Engage in initiatives that protect and enhance local environments  
   - Collaborate with stakeholders on environmental conservation projects  

6. **Transparency and Accountability**  
   - Be open about our business practices and their community impact  
   - Respond promptly to community concerns and grievances  
   - Review and report regularly on community engagement efforts  

## Responsibilities

- **Management**: Ensure implementation of this policy and provide necessary resources  
- **All Employees**: Understand and follow this policy in all community interactions  
- **Community Relations Department/Officer**: Coordinate engagement initiatives and monitor impact  

## Compliance and Review

- Adhere to all relevant laws, regulations, and community standards  
- Review this policy regularly to maintain effectiveness and relevance  

## Approval

This policy is approved by the executive management of **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, demonstrating our commitment to responsible and positive community engagement.
