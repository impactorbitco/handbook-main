## Overview

This document outlines the policy for managing cash within the organization, ensuring compliance with established standards and promoting ethical practices. The goal of this policy is to provide clear guidance on the strategic direction of cash management while adhering to the principles of information security and compliance management.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish consistent practices for cash handling, safeguarding organizational assets, and ensuring accountability. This document serves as a Level 1 strategic policy, aligning with the organization's commitment to ethical practices and compliance with ISO 37301 standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and stakeholders involved in cash management processes. It is designed to align with the principles outlined in the ISO Group A.5.1 - Policies for Information Security, ensuring that cash management practices are secure and compliant.

## Key Principles

1. **Ethical Conduct**

    All cash management activities must be conducted with the highest ethical standards, in line with the organization's Management Functions for Ethics.

2. **Information Security**

    Cash management processes must comply with the standards outlined in ISO Group A.5.1 to ensure the security of financial information.

3. **Compliance**

    The organization is committed to adhering to ISO 37301: Compliance Management System, ensuring that all cash management practices meet regulatory and legal requirements.

4. **Accountability**

    Employees and stakeholders involved in cash management are accountable for their actions and must adhere to the established procedures outlined in this policy.
