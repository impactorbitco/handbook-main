*Last updated: July 2025*

## üîç Scope

This policy covers all forms of charitable giving by Green Orbit Digital, including:

- Surplus product redistribution
- Monetary donations
- Employee volunteering and pro bono work
- Sponsorship of events, education programmes, or research initiatives
- Collaborative digital campaigns or in-kind services

## ‚úÖ Donation Criteria

To ensure alignment with our values and maximise impact, all supported charities and partners must meet **at least one of the following four core criteria**, with preference given to those meeting multiple:

### 1. üå± Local Impact

We prioritise projects and partners that:

- Operate in or benefit **Leicester and the East Midlands**
- Directly support disadvantaged or underserved UK communities
- Collaborate with local councils, schools, or sustainability alliances

### 2. ‚ôªÔ∏è Carbon Reduction / Environmental Stewardship

Charities must demonstrate:

- A measurable contribution to carbon reduction, biodiversity, or waste minimisation
- Alignment with **SDGs 12 (Responsible Consumption), 13 (Climate Action), 15 (Life on Land)**
- Ability to integrate or utilise **space-based data** or **digital reporting** for environmental monitoring (preferred)

### 3. üõ∞Ô∏è STEM Education & Innovation

We support organisations that:

- Promote access to **STEM careers**, especially for underrepresented groups
- Provide **space industry** outreach, training, or mentoring (e.g. UKSEDS)
- Facilitate **open data**, **civic tech**, or **research dissemination**

### 4. üåê Digital Inclusion

We prioritise:

- Initiatives providing access to **devices**, **connectivity**, or **digital skills**
- Refurbishment or redistribution of IT equipment
- Charities tackling **digital poverty**, particularly in education or social care

---

## üö´ Exclusion Criteria

We will not fund or donate to:

- Organisations that fail to meet transparency or regulatory standards (e.g. unregistered entities)
- Charities promoting political, discriminatory, or religious agendas
- Initiatives that conflict with Green Orbit‚Äôs sustainability commitments or digital ethics

## üìä Evaluation & Reporting

We will assess all donations for:

- **Alignment** with the four criteria (scoring rubric applied)
- **Impact potential**: measurable outcomes (CO‚ÇÇ saved, students trained, devices delivered, etc.)
- **Equity and inclusion**: who benefits and how equitably

Annual reporting will summarise:

- Donations made (type, value, recipient)
- SDGs addressed
- Local vs national/global impact
- Environmental and social outcomes
