## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide employees with a clear framework for requesting flexible working arrangements. Earthwave is committed to promoting a fair working environment that supports the diverse needs of its employees, ensuring a balance between business operations and employee well-being.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees of Earthwave who wish to request flexible working arrangements, irrespective of their role, tenure, or location. It aligns with the principles outlined in ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management and supports Earthwave's commitment to fair working practices.

## Policy Statement

Flexible working arrangements are a key component of Earthwave's commitment to fostering a supportive and inclusive workplace. Employees are encouraged to discuss and request flexible working arrangements that best suit their individual circumstances while meeting the operational requirements of the organization.

## Eligibility

All employees are eligible to submit a request for flexible working, regardless of their position or length of service.

## Procedure for Requesting Flexible Working

1. **Submission of Request**:
    - Employees should submit their request in writing to their line manager or the Human Resources department.
    - The request should include details of the proposed arrangement, reasons for the request, and any anticipated impact on business operations.
2. **Review Process**:
    - The request will be reviewed in accordance with the organization's management functions under Human Resources (Mgt code: HR).
    - Requests will be assessed based on operational feasibility, business needs, and alignment with Earthwave's Fair Working Policy.
3. **Outcome Notification**:
    - Employees will be notified of the outcome of their request within a reasonable timeframe.
    - If a request is denied, the employee will be provided with clear reasons and may have the opportunity to discuss alternative arrangements.

## Responsibilities

- **Employees**:
    - Submit requests in a timely and detailed manner.
    - Ensure that the proposed arrangements do not compromise their work commitments.
- **Line Managers**:
    - Consider requests impartially and in alignment with Earthwave's policies and ISO standards.
    - Work collaboratively with employees to explore feasible solutions.
- **Human Resources**:
    - Oversee the implementation of this policy and ensure compliance with relevant standards and regulations.
    - Maintain records of flexible working requests and decisions.

## Alignment with ISO Standards

This policy aligns with ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management and ISO Group A5.1 - Policies for Information Security, ensuring that employee well-being and organizational security are prioritized.
