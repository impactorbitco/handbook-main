## Purpose

This policy outlines the guidelines and requirements for the use of computer equipment outside of the workplace to ensure the security and integrity of company information and systems. It supports the organization's commitment to information security as described in ISO/IEC 27001 standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who have access to company-provided computer equipment and use it outside the physical workplace.

## Policy Statement

The organization is committed to maintaining the security of its information systems and protecting sensitive data. Employees and authorized users must adhere to the following requirements when using computer equipment away from the workplace.

### Requirements

1. **Authorization and Access Control**
    - Only authorized personnel may use company-provided computer equipment.
    - Access to equipment and systems must comply with the organization's access control policies.
2. **Physical Security**
    - Computer equipment must be stored securely when not in use.
    - Avoid leaving devices unattended in public or unsecured environments.
    - Report any loss or theft of equipment immediately to the Security team.
3. **Information Security**
    - Users must follow all policies for information security as outlined in ISMS/A5.1/POL/948.
    - Do not store sensitive information on personal or unauthorized devices.
    - Use encryption and secure connections (e.g., VPNs) when accessing company systems remotely.
4. **Usage Guidelines**
    - Equipment provided by the company must only be used for business purposes.
    - Follow the organization's acceptable use policies when accessing systems and data.
5. **Maintenance and Updates**
    - Keep all software and systems updated with the latest security patches.
    - Do not install unauthorized software on company-provided equipment.
6. **Incident Reporting**
    - Report any security incidents, breaches, or suspicious activity to the Security team immediately.

## Responsibility

- **Employees** must comply with this policy and ensure the secure use of equipment outside the workplace.
- **Management Functions (Security)**, identified as SEC, are responsible for enforcing this policy and providing necessary training and guidance.
- **Owner (Ryan Laird)** is responsible for maintaining and updating this policy.
