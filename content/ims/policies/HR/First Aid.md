# First Aid Policy

## Introduction

This First Aid Policy is established as part of the organization's commitment to ensuring the health and safety of all employees and stakeholders. It aligns with the requirements of ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management and supports the organization's strategic direction under the Health & Safety management function.

The purpose of this policy is to provide guidance on the provision of first aid in the workplace, ensuring prompt and effective care for individuals in the event of injury or illness.

## Objectives

The objectives of this policy are to:

1. Ensure compliance with ISO 45001 standards and other applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
2. Provide clear guidelines for managing first aid in the workplace.
3. Promote a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors across all locations where the organization operates. It outlines the responsibilities, resources, and procedures required to ensure effective first aid management.

## Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for maintaining and updating this policy to ensure relevance and compliance with applicable standards.
- **Management Functions**: The Health & Safety team is responsible for implementing and monitoring adherence to this policy.
- **All employees**: Employees are required to familiarize themselves with the first aid procedures and report any incidents promptly.

## Key Provisions

1. **First Aid Resources**:
    - Adequate first aid kits must be provided at all work locations and maintained regularly.
    - Designated first aid personnel must be trained and certified to provide assistance when needed.
2. **Incident Reporting**:
    - All first aid incidents must be reported promptly and documented for review and improvement purposes.
    - Incident reports must be filed in accordance with the organization's procedures.
3. **Training**:
    - Regular first aid training will be provided to ensure designated personnel remain certified and confident in their abilities.
    - Awareness sessions may be conducted for all employees to provide basic knowledge of first aid.
4. **Review and Improvement**:
    - This policy will be reviewed periodically as part of the organization's continuous improvement process.
    - Feedback from first aid incidents and reviews will be used to enhance the policy and procedures.
