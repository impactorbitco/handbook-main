## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines to prevent, address, and manage incidents of workplace violence to ensure the safety and well-being of all employees, contractors, and visitors. It aligns with the standards set forth in ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors to the company, regardless of their role or location. It is applicable to all company-related activities and facilities.

## Definitions

- **Workplace Violence**: Any act or threat of physical violence, harassment, intimidation, or other disruptive behavior that occurs in the workplace.
- **Employee**: Any person employed by the company, including full-time, part-time, and temporary staff.
- **Visitor**: Any individual present on company premises who is not an employee or contractor.

## Policy Statement

The company is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. Workplace violence of any kind will not be tolerated, and all incidents will be taken seriously and addressed promptly. This policy is designed to prevent workplace violence, outline reporting mechanisms, and provide resources for employees.

## Responsibilities

### Management

- Promote a culture of safety and respect.
- Ensure employees are aware of this policy and understand their roles in preventing workplace violence.
- Respond promptly to all reports of workplace violence.
- Conduct regular risk assessments and update this policy as required.

### Employees

- Report any incidents of workplace violence to their manager or Human Resources.
- Refrain from engaging in any behaviors that could be considered workplace violence.
- Participate in training sessions on workplace violence prevention.

### Human Resources

- Maintain confidentiality when addressing incidents of workplace violence.
- Provide training and resources to employees regarding workplace violence prevention.
- Investigate all reported incidents and take appropriate action.

## Reporting and Investigation

1. **Reporting**: Employees should report incidents of workplace violence to their immediate manager or Human Resources as soon as possible. Reports can be made verbally or in writing.
2. **Investigation**: All reports will be investigated thoroughly, fairly, and promptly. The company will take appropriate measures to protect the confidentiality of those involved.
3. **Resolution**: If an incident is confirmed, the company will take corrective action, which may include disciplinary measures, up to and including termination of employment, and/or legal action.

## Preventative Measures

- Conduct regular training for employees on workplace violence prevention.
- Implement security measures where necessary, such as controlled access to facilities.
- Foster a workplace culture that emphasizes respect, inclusion, and open communication.

## Standards and Compliance

This policy is in compliance with ISO 45001: Occupational Health and Safety Management standards. It also adheres to the company’s strategic direction as outlined in the document hierarchy (Level 1).

## Monitoring and Review
