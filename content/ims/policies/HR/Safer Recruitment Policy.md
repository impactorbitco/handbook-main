**Introduction:**
At Green Orbit Digital, we are committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of all individuals involved in our operations. Our Safer Recruitment Policy outlines our procedures and principles to promote a safe and secure recruitment process, safeguarding both our employees and the integrity of our organization.

**Policy Statement:**
Green Orbit Digital is dedicated to maintaining a recruitment process that prioritizes the safety and welfare of all applicants, employees, and stakeholders. We adhere to legal requirements and best practices to prevent potential risks and ensure transparency and fairness throughout the recruitment lifecycle.

**Key Principles:**

1. **Equality and Diversity:** We embrace diversity and promote equality in our recruitment practices, ensuring that all candidates are treated fairly and without discrimination based on factors such as race, gender, age, disability, sexual orientation, religion, or nationality.
2. **Transparency:** We provide clear and comprehensive information to applicants about the recruitment process, job requirements, and expectations. All communications regarding vacancies, assessments, and decisions are conducted transparently and honestly.
3. **Safeguarding:** Safeguarding the welfare of vulnerable individuals, including children and adults at risk, is paramount. We conduct appropriate checks and assessments to ensure that candidates are suitable for roles involving interaction with vulnerable populations.
4. **Competency-Based Selection:** Our recruitment process focuses on assessing candidates' skills, qualifications, and relevant experience based on job-related criteria. We use competency-based interviews and assessments to evaluate candidates objectively and fairly.
5. **Background Checks:** To verify the authenticity of candidates' qualifications, employment history, and other relevant information, we conduct thorough background checks as appropriate for the role and in compliance with legal requirements.
6. **References:** We obtain and validate references from previous employers or other relevant sources to assess candidates' suitability and integrity for the role.
7. **Training and Awareness:** All staff involved in the recruitment process receive training on safer recruitment practices, including recognizing signs of potential risks or safeguarding concerns.

**Responsibilities:**

- **HR Department:** The Human Resources department is responsible for overseeing and implementing the Safer Recruitment Policy, ensuring compliance with legal requirements and best practices.
- **Managers and Supervisors:** Hiring managers and supervisors are responsible for adhering to the policy principles and participating in recruitment processes with integrity and transparency.
- **Recruitment Panel Members:** Individuals involved in recruitment panels must conduct assessments objectively, ensure fairness and equality, and promptly report any safeguarding concerns to the HR department.

**Compliance and Review:**
This policy is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect changes in legislation, best practices, and organizational needs. Non-compliance with the Safer Recruitment Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

**Conclusion:**
By adhering to the principles outlined in this policy, Green Orbit Digital demonstrates its commitment to promoting a safe, fair, and inclusive recruitment process that upholds the welfare of all individuals involved.

# **Safer Recruitment Policy**

# **Purpose**

**The Safer Recruitment Policy outlines the strategic approach and mandatory processes for ensuring that recruitment practices within the organization align with information security standards, compliance requirements, and human resource management principles. This policy is designed to streamline recruitment processes, enhance employer branding, and maintain compliance with ISO standards.**

# **Scope**

**This policy applies to all recruitment activities across the organization, including but not limited to job postings, candidate selection, background checks, and onboarding procedures. It is relevant to all departments and personnel involved in recruitment or hiring processes.**

# **Objectives**

1. **Ensure recruitment processes are conducted in a manner that prioritizes information security and compliance.**
2. **Enhance employer branding to attract top talent while maintaining high standards for integrity and professionalism.**
3. **Align recruitment practices with ISO 37301: Compliance Management System and ISO Group A.5.1 - Policies for Information Security.**

# **Policy Statement**

**The organization is committed to fostering safe and secure recruitment practices that adhere to the following principles:**

1. **Transparency: All stages of the recruitment process will be conducted with clear communication and adherence to established policies.**
2. **Compliance: Recruitment practices will meet the requirements of ISO 37301: Compliance Management System and ISO Group A.5.1 standards, ensuring that information security is maintained throughout the process.**
3. **Verification: All candidates will undergo appropriate background checks and verification steps to confirm their qualifications and suitability for the role.**
4. **Efficiency: Recruitment processes will be streamlined to reduce inefficiencies while maintaining thoroughness and care.**
5. **Employer Branding: Efforts will be made to enhance the organization’s reputation as a desirable workplace, ensuring that recruitment practices reflect the company’s values and mission.**

# **Responsibilities**

- **Management Functions: The Human Resources department (HR) is responsible for implementing and monitoring this policy. The HR team will oversee recruitment processes and ensure adherence to the guidelines outlined.**
- **Policy Owner: Ryan Laird is the designated owner of this policy and will ensure that it remains up-to-date and relevant.**
- **Compliance Verification: Recruitment practices will be verified against ISO 37301 and CMS/A5.1 standards to ensure full compliance.**

# **Implementation**

1. **Streamline Recruitment Processes: Recruitment workflows will be reviewed and optimized to remove unnecessary steps and improve efficiency.**
2. **Enhance Employer Branding: Initiatives will be launched to promote the organization as an employer of choice, including improved job descriptions, recruitment marketing, and social media campaigns.**
3. **Background Checks: All candidates will undergo rigorous checks to verify their credentials, work history, and suitability for the role.**
4. **Training: HR personnel involved in recruitment will receive training on compliance requirements and information security standards.**

# **Monitoring and Review**

**This policy will be reviewed periodically to ensure its effectiveness and alignment with organizational goals and compliance requirements. Feedback from hiring managers and candidates will be incorporated into future revisions.**
