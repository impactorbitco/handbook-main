# Impact Orbit Creative Group  
## Safer Recruitment Policy

---

## 1. Introduction

Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to safeguarding the safety, wellbeing, and rights of all individuals involved in our activities. This Safer Recruitment Policy sets out the principles and procedures we follow to ensure that our recruitment and selection processes are robust, ethical, and aligned with best practice.

The policy supports our commitment to building a trusted organisation that operates responsibly across creative, digital, and impact-led work, while protecting employees, partners, clients, and the wider community.

---

## 2. Policy Statement

Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to operating a recruitment process that prioritises safety, integrity, and fairness. We take appropriate steps to minimise risk, prevent harm, and ensure that individuals appointed to roles are suitable, competent, and aligned with our values.

All recruitment activities are conducted in compliance with relevant legislation and guidance, and in a way that promotes transparency, consistency, and accountability throughout the recruitment lifecycle.

---

## 3. Key Principles

### 3.1 Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion

Impact Orbit Creative Group is an equal opportunities organisation. We are committed to inclusive recruitment practices and ensure that no applicant is treated less favourably on the basis of:

- Race or ethnicity  
- Gender or gender identity  
- Age  
- Disability or neurodiversity  
- Sexual orientation  
- Religion or belief  
- Nationality or socio-economic background  

Selection decisions are based solely on merit, role requirements, and organisational fit.

---

### 3.2 Transparency and Fairness

- Job roles, responsibilities, and selection criteria are clearly defined and communicated.
- Applicants are provided with accurate information about the recruitment process and expectations.
- Decisions are made fairly, consistently, and supported by documented evidence.

---

### 3.3 Safeguarding

Safeguarding is a core consideration within recruitment. Where roles involve contact with children, young people, or adults at risk, Impact Orbit Creative Group will:

- Assess the level of risk associated with the role  
- Carry out appropriate safeguarding checks in line with legal and regulatory requirements  
- Ensure suitability is assessed alongside skills and experience  

---

### 3.4 Competency-Based Selection

Our recruitment process is designed to assess candidates objectively using job-related criteria. This may include:

- Competency-based interviews  
- Skills assessments or practical tasks  
- Structured scoring frameworks  

This approach ensures consistency, reduces bias, and supports fair decision-making.

---

### 3.5 Background and Right-to-Work Checks

Where appropriate and lawful, Impact Orbit Creative Group will undertake checks to verify:

- Identity and right to work  
- Qualifications and professional memberships  
- Employment history  

All checks are proportionate to the role and carried out in compliance with data protection and employment law.

---

### 3.6 References

- References will normally be obtained prior to appointment.
- Referees should be suitable and able to comment on the candidateâ€™s performance, conduct, and suitability for the role.
- Any concerns arising from references will be reviewed and assessed before a final decision is made.

---

### 3.7 Training and Awareness

- Staff involved in recruitment and selection receive appropriate guidance and training on safer recruitment principles.
- Training includes awareness of safeguarding responsibilities and recognising potential risks during the recruitment process.

---

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

### 4.1 Human Resources / People Function

The HR or People function is responsible for:

- Overseeing implementation of this policy  
- Ensuring compliance with employment and safeguarding legislation  
- Providing guidance to hiring managers  
- Maintaining appropriate recruitment records  

---

### 4.2 Hiring Managers and Supervisors

Hiring managers are responsible for:

- Applying this policy consistently  
- Conducting recruitment activities with integrity and professionalism  
- Ensuring decisions are fair, evidence-based, and documented  
- Escalating any safeguarding or suitability concerns  

---

### 4.3 Recruitment Panel Members

Individuals involved in interviews or assessments must:

- Act objectively and without bias  
- Promote equality and fairness  
- Maintain confidentiality  
- Report any safeguarding or conduct concerns promptly  

---

## 5. Compliance and Review

This policy is reviewed regularly to ensure it remains compliant with legislation, reflects best practice, and supports the evolving needs of Impact Orbit Creative Group.

Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or engagement.

---

## 6. Conclusion

By adhering to this Safer Recruitment Policy, Impact Orbit Creative Group demonstrates its commitment to responsible, ethical, and inclusive recruitment practices. We aim to build a workforce that reflects our values, supports our mission, and contributes positively to a safe and trusted organisational culture.
