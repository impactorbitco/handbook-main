## Introduction

This document outlines the policy for the management and use of work equipment and machinery within the organization. It aligns with the strategic direction of the company and supports sustainability goals through environmentally responsible practices. The policy complies with ISO 14001 standards for Environmental Management Systems as well as ISO Group A.5.1 for Information Security Policies.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to ensure that all work equipment and machinery are managed responsibly and sustainably to minimize environmental impact while maintaining operational efficiency. It also aims to protect the safety and well-being of employees and stakeholders, ensure compliance with relevant standards, and support the organization's overall strategic objectives.

## Scope

This policy applies to all work equipment and machinery utilized within the organization, covering procurement, operation, maintenance, and disposal. It is relevant to all employees, contractors, and third parties who interact with or manage such equipment.

## Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for the development, implementation, and review of this policy.
- **Management Functions**: The sustainability team is tasked with ensuring adherence to the policy and integrating it into the company’s environmental management strategies.
- **Employees and Contractors**: All personnel must comply with this policy and report any issues or non-conformities related to work equipment and machinery.

## Principles

1. **Sustainability**: Prioritize the use of environmentally friendly equipment and practices. Ensure that machinery is energy-efficient and contributes to minimizing the organization’s environmental footprint.
2. **Safety and Compliance**: Follow all relevant safety regulations and standards. Maintain adherence to ISO 14001 and EMS/A5.1/POL/930 guidelines.
3. **Maintenance and Inspection**: Regularly inspect and maintain equipment to ensure optimal functionality and safety.
4. **Disposal**: Dispose of equipment and machinery responsibly, in accordance with environmental standards and regulations.

## Implementation

- **Procurement**: All new equipment and machinery purchases must align with sustainability objectives and ISO standards.
- **Training**: Employees must receive training to operate machinery safely and efficiently in accordance with this policy.
- **Monitoring and Review**: Regular audits and reviews will be conducted to ensure the effectiveness of this policy.

## Verification and Publishing

This policy has been verified and is currently in the "In Review" stage, with a status of Version 1.1. It will be publicly visible upon completion of the review process. The policy’s priority level is classified as Low, and it is not currently part of the Company Handbook or Archive.

## Conclusion

The organization is committed to the responsible management of work equipment and machinery as part of its overarching sustainability and strategic goals. Compliance with this policy ensures operational efficiency, environmental responsibility, and the safety of all stakeholders.

For further information, please contact Ryan Laird, the policy owner.
