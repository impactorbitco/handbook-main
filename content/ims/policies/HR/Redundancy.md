## Overview

This document outlines the redundancy policy of the organization, providing a strategic direction for managing redundancy in alignment with the organization's compliance and information security standards. This policy is classified as a Level 1 document under the hierarchy of strategic direction and is governed by the ISO 37301: Compliance Management System and ISO Group A.5.1 - Policies for Information Security.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a framework for handling redundancy situations within the organization in a manner that is fair, transparent, and compliant with legal and regulatory requirements. It seeks to balance organizational efficiency with employee rights and well-being.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees across all departments under the management function of Human Resources.

## Policy Details

### Key Principles

1. **Fairness and Transparency**: Redundancy decisions will be made based on objective criteria and communicated clearly to the affected parties.
2. **Compliance**: All redundancy processes will comply with ISO standards, including ISO 37301 for compliance management and ISO Group A.5.1 for information security policies.
3. **Support for Affected Employees**: The organization will provide support, such as career counseling and severance packages, to employees impacted by redundancy.

### Process

1. **Consultation**: Employees and their representatives will be consulted before any redundancy decisions are finalized.
2. **Selection**: An objective selection process will be used to determine which roles are redundant.
3. **Notification**: Affected employees will be notified in writing with clear explanations for the decision.
4. **Support**: The organization will offer support to help employees transition, such as job placement services or retraining opportunities.

### Responsibilities

- **Policy Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for maintaining and updating this policy.
- **Human Resources Management**: Responsible for implementing the policy and ensuring compliance with relevant legal and regulatory frameworks.
- **Employees**: Expected to cooperate fully with the redundancy process and provide necessary information when requested.
