# Screening Policy (Pre-Employment Checks)

## Overview

This document outlines the pre-employment screening policy for the organization, ensuring that all potential hires are subject to appropriate checks to maintain the security and integrity of the company. This policy is aligned with the ISO/IEC 27001: Information Security Management System standard and follows the Annex A6 Control Group - people guidelines.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide a framework for conducting pre-employment checks that balance the need for thorough verification with respect for individual privacy and fairness. It is a key component of our strategic direction to ensure that all employees meet the requirements for their roles while adhering to our information security standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all candidates being considered for employment within the organization, regardless of role, level, or geographical location. It is applicable to full-time, part-time, temporary, and contract positions.

## Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for overseeing the development, implementation, and review of this policy.
- **Hiring Managers**: Responsible for ensuring compliance with this policy during the recruitment process.
- **Human Resources**: Responsible for coordinating the pre-employment checks and maintaining records.

## Screening Process

1. **Identity Verification**:
    - Confirm the identity of candidates through government-issued identification.
2. **Employment History Checks**:
    - Verify the candidate's previous employment history, including dates of employment and roles held.
3. **Education Verification**:
    - Validate the educational qualifications listed in the candidate’s application.
4. **Criminal Record Checks**:
    - Conduct criminal background checks in accordance with local laws and regulations.
5. **Reference Checks**:
    - Obtain professional references from previous employers or supervisors.
6. **Right to Work**:
    - Verify that the candidate has the legal right to work in the country of employment.
7. **Additional Checks**:
    - For roles with heightened security requirements, additional checks such as credit history, drug testing, or security clearance may be conducted.

## Compliance with Standards

This policy adheres to the ISO/IEC 27001 standard, specifically addressing the Information Security Management System (ISMS) requirements under Annex A6 Control Group - people.

## Record Keeping

All records obtained during the pre-employment screening process will be maintained securely and in compliance with applicable data protection regulations. Records will only be accessible to authorized personnel and will be retained for a period in line with organizational and legal requirements.
