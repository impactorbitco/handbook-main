## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a structured approach to managing and evaluating work performance within the organization. This policy aims to ensure consistency, fairness, and alignment with the organization’s strategic goals, while adhering to the principles of the ISO 9001 Quality Management System and other relevant standards.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees within the organization and outlines the processes for performance management, including goal setting, performance evaluations, and feedback mechanisms. It is designed for use by Human Resources and management to support the continuous improvement of employee performance.

## Policy Statement

The organization is committed to fostering a high-performance culture where employees are supported in achieving their individual and collective goals. Effective performance management ensures alignment with the organization's strategic direction and promotes accountability, transparency, and professional growth.

## Objectives

- To provide clear expectations for employee performance and behavior
- To ensure formal performance evaluations are conducted regularly and include measurable goals, including social and environmental objectives
- To establish a consistent framework for providing constructive feedback and addressing performance issues
- To support career development and continuous improvement for all employees

## Responsibilities

### Human Resources

- Develop and maintain performance management processes in alignment with ISO 9001 standards.
- Provide training and guidance to managers on conducting performance evaluations.
- Ensure all managers complete formal written performance evaluations annually, including the consideration of social and environmental goals.

### Managers

- Set clear, measurable goals for their teams that align with the organization’s strategic objectives.
- Conduct regular performance evaluations and provide constructive feedback to employees.
- Address performance issues promptly and fairly, and ensure documentation is completed accurately.

### Employees

- Understand and adhere to individual performance expectations.
- Actively participate in performance evaluation and feedback sessions.
- Strive for continuous improvement and professional growth.

## Performance Evaluation Process

1. **Goal Setting**

    At the beginning of each evaluation period, managers will work with employees to set clear, measurable, and achievable goals. These goals will align with the organization's strategic objectives and may include social and environmental targets.

2. **Ongoing Feedback**

    Managers are encouraged to provide regular feedback to employees to recognize achievements and address any performance issues proactively.

3. **Formal Evaluations**

    All employees will undergo a formal performance evaluation at least once per year. These evaluations will include:

    - Assessment of goal achievement
    - Feedback on strengths and areas for improvement
    - Discussion of social and environmental contributions, where applicable
    - Development of a plan for future performance and career growth
4. **Documentation**

    All performance evaluations and related documentation will be retained and managed by Human Resources in accordance with organizational policies and applicable data protection standards.

## Compliance and Verification

This policy is aligned with the ISO 9001 Quality Management System and supports compliance with information security policies under ISO Group A.5.1. The implementation and effectiveness of this policy will be verified through internal audits and reviews.
