# **Introduction**

At [Your Business Name], we believe in the continuous professional development of our employees as a key component of our business success. This policy outlines our commitment to providing training and development opportunities that enhance the skills, knowledge, and competencies of our employees.

**Scope**

This policy applies to all employees of [Your Business Name].

**Our Commitment**

1. **Training Opportunities:** We are committed to offering a range of training opportunities that support the professional growth and skill development of our employees. This includes:

- On-the-job training, workshops, and seminars.
- Online courses and e-learning modules.
- External training programs and conferences.

2. **Career Development:** We support the career advancement of our employees by:

- Providing clear career paths and progression opportunities.
- Encouraging cross-functional training and job rotation.
- Offering mentorship and coaching programs.

3. **Performance Management:** Training and development are integral to our performance management process. We will:

- Regularly assess the training needs of employees in line with their roles and career aspirations.
- Include training and development goals in performance evaluations.
- Use performance reviews to identify further training needs and opportunities.

4. **Employee-Led Learning:** We encourage our employees to take an active role in their own development by:

- Supporting self-directed learning initiatives.
- Providing access to learning resources and materials.
- Recognizing and rewarding self-improvement efforts.

5. **Budget and Resources:** We are committed to allocating appropriate resources and budget for training and development activities to ensure:

- Availability of quality training programs.
- Accessibility for all employees to participate in training initiatives.

6. **Inclusivity and Accessibility**: Our training and development programs are inclusive and accessible to all employees, regardless of their role, level, or location.

**Responsibilities**

- Management is responsible for endorsing and promoting this policy and allocating resources for training initiatives.
- Human Resources is responsible for the development, implementation, and monitoring of training programs.
- Employees are encouraged to actively participate in training and development opportunities and provide feedback.

**Monitoring and Evaluation**

- We will regularly monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of our training and development programs.
- Feedback from employees will be used to continually improve training content and delivery methods.

**Review and Update**

- This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure it remains relevant and effective.
- Updates will be made to reflect changes in our business needs and industry trends.

**Approval**

This policy has been approved by the highest level of management at [Your Business Name], reflecting our commitment to the ongoing development of our employees.

**Contact Information**

For more information or questions about this policy, please contact [Contact Person/Department].

# Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish the framework for employee training and development within the company, ensuring that all full-time employees have access to professional development opportunities that align with both strategic organizational goals and individual growth objectives.

# Scope

This policy applies to all full-time employees across all departments and management levels. It aims to promote continuous learning, skill enhancement, and knowledge sharing to support the company's strategic direction as aligned with ISO 30401: Knowledge Management System (KMS).

# Objectives

1. Provide employees with access to training and development programs that enhance their skills and competencies.
2. Ensure training programs align with the company’s strategic goals and priorities, including social and environmental issues material to the organization.
3. Foster a culture of continuous learning and development to improve employee performance and engagement.
4. Regularly evaluate and update training programs to remain relevant and effective.

# Policy Details

- **Commitment to Development**: The company is committed to providing relevant training and development opportunities for all full-time employees. This includes technical training, soft skills development, and leadership programs.
- **Access and Inclusivity**: All training opportunities will be accessible to employees without discrimination and will be designed to meet diverse learning needs.
- **Alignment with Strategic Goals**: Training programs will be developed and implemented to support the organization's strategic direction (Hierarchy: Level 1 – Strategic Direction) and human resources management functions (Mgt code: HR).

- **Employees**: Actively participate in training programs and apply the acquired knowledge and skills to their roles.
- **HR Department**: Develop, implement, and monitor training and development programs. Maintain training records and assess the effectiveness of programs.

**Introduction**

This is the staff data protection training policy for INSERT BUSINESS NAME.

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is based around six principles of handling of personal data. Data privacy and security are a key part of the principles. As a business we want to ensure that all our employees are aware of the importance of the rules around data protection.

**Scope**

To that end all employees that handle personal information of individuals must have a basic understanding of the GDPR and other relevant data protection laws. Staff with duties such as computer and internet security, marketing and database management may need specialist training to make them aware of particular data protection requirements in their work area.

**Training topics**

Training will include but is not limited topics such as:

- Identifying the different categories of data;
- An understanding of the 6 principles of GDPR;
- An understanding of the lawful bases for processing data under GDRP;
- An awareness of the 8 rights that individuals have;
- Knowing who to contact in relation to data protection queries in your business;
- How to identify and process a Subject Access Request;
- How to identify a data breach for notification purposes and how and when to report this;
- The internal policies and procedures that the business has to comply with the GDPR.

# Training and Awareness Requirements

Green Orbit Digital ensures effective training and awareness across the organization through structured programs and continuous learning initiatives.

## Competence Development

- Define Knowledge Management Competencies including data stewardship, structured documentation, process mapping, information security awareness, and reflective learning
- Integrate competence requirements into role descriptions to ensure all team members understand their knowledge management responsibilities
- Maintain Skills Matrix to identify gaps and plan targeted training investments

## Learning Opportunities

- Internal knowledge-sharing sessions through retrospectives and lunch & learns
- Micro-trainings and comprehensive onboarding guides for new staff
- External professional development in ISO standards, sustainability reporting, and digital tools
- Regular competence assessments through periodic reviews and 360-degree feedback

## Awareness Building

- Communicate KMS purpose and benefits during onboarding and quarterly updates
- Share success stories demonstrating knowledge management impact on projects and efficiency
- Embed knowledge sharing reminders in project workflows and review processes
- Recognize and reward active knowledge-sharing behaviors

## Communication Channels

- Utilize Notion as primary internal communication and documentation platform
- Maintain dedicated spaces for policies, procedures, playbooks, and learning archives
- Implement real-time collaboration tools for knowledge exchange
- Conduct quarterly check-ins to review knowledge strategy progress
