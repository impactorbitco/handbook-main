### Introduction

At Impact Orbit Creative Group, we are committed to maintaining a safe, healthy, and productive work environment for all employees. Our Alcohol and Substance Misuse Policy outlines our approach to addressing alcohol and substance misuse in the workplace to ensure the well-being and safety of our employees and the integrity of our organization.

### Policy Statement

1. **Prohibition of Alcohol and Substance Misuse**: The consumption, possession, distribution, or being under the influence of alcohol or illegal substances during work hours, while on company premises, or while representing Impact Orbit Creative Group in any capacity is strictly prohibited.
2. **Prescription Medication**: The use of prescription medication that may impair cognitive or physical abilities is permitted only if prescribed by a licensed healthcare provider and does not pose a safety risk to the individual or others in the workplace.
3. **Drug-Free Workplace**: Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to maintaining a drug-free workplace. Any employee found to be using, possessing, or distributing illegal drugs on company premises will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

### Responsibilities

1. **Employee Responsibilities**:
    - Employees are expected to report to work in a fit condition to perform their duties safely and effectively.
    - Employees must refrain from consuming alcohol or using illegal substances during work hours or while representing the company.
    - Employees who are prescribed medication that may affect their ability to perform their job duties should notify their supervisor and Human Resources department.
2. **Manager and Supervisor Responsibilities**:
    - Managers and supervisors are responsible for monitoring employee behavior and addressing any signs of alcohol or substance misuse promptly and confidentially.
    - Managers and supervisors should provide support and guidance to employees who may be struggling with alcohol or substance misuse issues and refer them to appropriate resources for assistance.
3. **Human Resources Responsibilities**:
    - The Human Resources department will provide training and education on alcohol and substance misuse awareness and prevention to all employees.
    - HR will maintain confidentiality and handle alcohol and substance misuse cases sensitively and impartially, following the company's disciplinary procedures as necessary.

### Consequences

1. **Disciplinary Action**: Violations of the Alcohol and Substance Misuse Policy will result in disciplinary action, which may include verbal warnings, written warnings, suspension, or termination of employment, depending on the severity of the offense and the employee's history of misconduct.
2. **Support and Assistance**: Employees who voluntarily seek assistance for alcohol or substance misuse issues will be provided with access to confidential support services, counseling, and rehabilitation programs to help them overcome their challenges and return to work successfully.

### Conclusion

Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to promoting a safe, healthy, and drug-free workplace for all employees. By adhering to the guidelines outlined in this policy, we aim to prevent alcohol and substance misuse, support employees in need of assistance, and maintain a positive and productive work environment for everyone.
