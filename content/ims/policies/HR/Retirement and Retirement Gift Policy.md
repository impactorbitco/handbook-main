## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide guidance on the organization's approach to retirement and retirement gifts. It aims to ensure that retiring employees are recognized for their contributions and that the process is conducted in a manner consistent with the company's ethical standards and commitment to social responsibility.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees of the organization who are approaching retirement or are retiring. It outlines the procedures for retirement recognition and the guidelines for providing retirement gifts.

## Policy Statement

The organization values the dedication and contributions of its employees. Retirement is a significant milestone, and the organization is committed to celebrating this event in a meaningful way. This policy ensures that retirement procedures align with the organization's values, including ethics, fairness, and social responsibility.

## Retirement Recognition

1. **Eligibility**: Employees who have reached the organization's defined retirement age or who are retiring after a minimum of [X] years of service are eligible for retirement recognition.
2. **Celebration**: The organization will host a retirement event or ceremony to honor the retiring employee's contributions. This may include a gathering, speeches, and the presentation of a retirement gift.
3. **Acknowledgment**: The employee's service and achievements will be formally acknowledged by management during the event.

## Retirement Gifts

1. **Guidelines**:
    - A retirement gift will be provided to the retiring employee as a token of appreciation.
    - The gift will be selected based on the employee’s preferences, years of service, and organizational budget.
    - Examples of gifts may include personalized items, plaques, or monetary gifts.
2. **Approval**:
    - All retirement gifts must be approved by the employee’s manager and align with the organization's budgetary policies.

## Roles and Responsibilities

1. **Management**:
    - Ensure that the retirement process is conducted in a respectful and professional manner.
    - Approve retirement celebrations and gifts.
2. **Human Resources (HR)**:
    - Coordinate the retirement event and ensure adherence to this policy.
    - Communicate with the retiring employee to understand their preferences for the celebration and gift.
3. **Employees**:
    - Provide feedback on the process, if applicable, to ensure continuous improvement in the retirement recognition program.

## Standards and Compliance

This policy aligns with:

- **ISO 26000: Social Responsibility Management System**: Ensuring the process is socially responsible and ethical.
