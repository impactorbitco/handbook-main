## Principles of Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion

At **Impact Orbit Creative Group**, we uphold equality, diversity, and inclusion (EDI) as fundamental values that guide our decisions, behaviours, and organisational culture. These principles are embedded across all aspects of our organisation, ensuring every individual is treated with dignity, respect, fairness, and equality.

We believe that diverse perspectives strengthen creativity, innovation, and impact, and we are committed to building an organisation where everyone can contribute fully and authentically.

---

## Commitment to Inclusive Workspaces

Impact Orbit Creative Group proudly champions diversity and inclusion. We are committed to creating a workplace where all staff, contractors, and collaborators are treated equitably and with respect.

Our commitment goes beyond policy statements—it is reflected in our leadership behaviours, operational practices, recruitment processes, and everyday interactions.

---

## Upholding a Respectful Workplace Culture

### Objective: Cultivating Inclusivity

Our foremost objective is to foster a workplace environment that is not only harmonious, but actively inclusive. We do not tolerate intimidation, bullying, harassment, discrimination, or any form of threatening or abusive behaviour.

Every individual has the right to work in an environment that is safe, supportive, and respectful.

**Actions:**

- Run regular awareness campaigns and inclusion initiatives  
- Deliver sensitivity, inclusion, and respect-at-work training  
- Promote shared responsibility for maintaining a respectful culture  

---

## Equal Opportunities for All

### Striving for Equality of Opportunity

We recognise and value the unique strengths that diverse backgrounds, experiences, skills, abilities, and ages bring to Impact Orbit Creative Group. Our commitment to equality includes maintaining a workplace free from harassment and discrimination, where individual potential is nurtured and enabled.

**Actions:**

- Participate in ongoing diversity and inclusion programmes  
- Provide mentorship, sponsorship, and talent development initiatives  
- Ensure equitable access to learning, progression, and leadership opportunities  

---

## A Welcoming Environment for All

### Inclusive Application and Engagement Processes

Impact Orbit Creative Group actively encourages applications from individuals passionate about creativity, sustainability, and impact—regardless of background or personal characteristics. We believe diversity fuels innovation and resilience.

**Actions:**

- Expand outreach to underrepresented and diverse communities  
- Collaborate with community organisations and networks  
- Review application, onboarding, and engagement processes for accessibility and fairness  

---

## Nurturing Career Development

### Supporting Diverse Career Paths

Beyond providing equal opportunity, we are committed to actively supporting the long-term development of our people. This includes ensuring access to benefits, opportunities, and resources is fair and equitable.

**Actions:**

- Deliver mentorship and coaching programmes  
- Offer skill-building workshops and professional development resources  
- Support flexible and non-linear career pathways  

---

## Merit-Based Recruitment Practices

### Building a Merit-Driven Team

Our recruitment and selection practices are merit-based. We prioritise skills, experience, enthusiasm, and potential over characteristics unrelated to role performance.

**Actions:**

- Use transparent, structured, and fair recruitment processes  
- Apply consistent selection criteria  
- Where appropriate, use blind or bias-reducing recruitment techniques  

---

## Openness and Continuous Learning

### EDI Policy Addendum: Learning, Openness, and Growth

Impact Orbit Creative Group recognises that diversity and inclusion are ongoing journeys. We foster a culture of openness, reflection, and continuous learning to deepen understanding and improve practice.

**Actions:**

- Deliver regular EDI training and workshops  
- Encourage open dialogue and safe discussion spaces  
- Actively seek, listen to, and act on feedback  
- Continuously review and improve policies and practices  

---

## Practical Actions to Foster Inclusion

We embed inclusion through a combination of leadership commitment, policy, and practice:

### Leadership Commitment
- Demonstrate visible commitment to EDI through actions and accountability  
- Set clear inclusion goals and track progress  

### Inclusive Recruitment
- Use inclusive language in job descriptions  
- Ensure diverse perspectives in hiring panels  
- Actively recruit from underrepresented communities  

### Training and Education
- Provide ongoing training on unconscious bias and inclusive leadership  
- Encourage open discussion and shared learning  

### Equitable Opportunities
- Ensure fairness in promotion, pay, and development  
- Offer mentorship and sponsorship for underrepresented groups  

### Employee Voice and Feedback
- Maintain confidential and anonymous feedback mechanisms  
- Act promptly on concerns raised  

### Accessible Workspaces
- Ensure physical and digital accessibility  
- Use inclusive language across communications  

### Celebrating Diversity
- Acknowledge cultural, religious, and personal milestones  
- Create opportunities for storytelling and shared experiences  

### Measuring Progress
- Monitor diversity and inclusion metrics  
- Use data and feedback to refine initiatives  

---

## Our Commitment

Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to:

- **Promoting Diversity:** Celebrating diversity as a source of strength and innovation  
- **Preventing Discrimination:** Addressing and preventing discrimination in all forms  
- **Educating and Informing:** Building awareness, empathy, and understanding  
- **Supporting Career Growth:** Providing equal access to development and progression  
- **Fostering an Inclusive Culture:** Respecting and valuing every individual  

By embracing these principles, we aim to create a workplace where diversity is celebrated and everyone feels empowered to contribute their best.

---

## Raising Concerns

Any concerns relating to equality, diversity, or inclusion are encouraged to be raised with leadership or through appropriate reporting channels. This ensures accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement in our commitment to an inclusive and equitable workplace.
