## Overview

This document outlines the Quality Training Policy within the organization, classified as a Level 1 strategic direction document within the Quality Management System (QMS). It is aligned with ISO 9001:2015 standards for quality management and aims to provide guidance for achieving sustained success through formal quality control mechanisms.

## Purpose

The primary purpose of this policy is to ensure that employees receive adequate and effective training on quality management principles and practices. This training aims to strengthen the organization's ability to maintain high-quality standards, adhere to ISO 9001 guidelines, and support sustained organizational success.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees within the organization, particularly those involved in quality management functions. It covers training on formal quality control mechanisms, ISO 9001:2015 requirements, and other related quality management practices.

## Objectives

- Equip employees with the knowledge and skills required to uphold the organization's quality management standards.
- Ensure compliance with ISO 9001:2015 and other relevant policies for quality and information security.
- Promote sustained organizational success through effective quality training practices.
- Address social and environmental issues material to the company as part of employee training, as outlined in the SBA ESG Self-Assessment.

## Roles and Responsibilities

- **Owner**: Ryan Laird is responsible for overseeing the development, implementation, and review of this policy.
- **Quality Management Functions**: The Quality team (Mgt code: QLT) is tasked with designing and delivering training programs, monitoring compliance, and identifying areas for improvement.
- **Employees**: All employees are required to participate in training programs as applicable to their roles and responsibilities.

## Training Content

The quality training program will include, but is not limited to:

1. **Formal Quality Control Mechanisms**: Training on tools and processes designed to ensure product and service quality.
2. **ISO 9001:2015 Standards**: Comprehensive guidance on adhering to the quality management system requirements.
3. **Sustained Success Practices**: Guidance on achieving long-term organizational success through quality-driven initiatives.
4. **Social and Environmental Issues**: Training will include material on how quality management intersects with the company’s social and environmental responsibilities.
