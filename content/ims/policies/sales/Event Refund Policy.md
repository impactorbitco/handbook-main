## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to provide clear guidelines regarding refund requests for events organized by the company. It ensures transparency, fairness, and alignment with the organization's ethical and compliance standards while addressing refund-related concerns effectively.

## Scope

This policy applies to all events organized by the company and is intended for use by event organizers, participants, and stakeholders. It aligns with ISO 37301: Compliance Management System and ISO A.5.1: Policies for Information Security.

## Policy Statement

The company is committed to maintaining ethical practices and fostering trust with event participants. Refund requests will be handled in a manner that is consistent, transparent, and fair, while prioritizing stakeholder satisfaction and compliance with relevant standards.

## Key Principles

1. **Transparency**: Refund terms and conditions will be clearly communicated to participants prior to event registration.
2. **Fairness**: Refund requests will be evaluated based on the outlined criteria and processed equitably.
3. **Timeliness**: All refund requests will be reviewed and processed within a reasonable timeframe.
4. **Sustainability**: The company will strive to minimize the environmental impact of events, including considerations for refunds and cancellations.
5. **Compliance**: All refund processes will adhere to ISO 37301 and ISO A.5.1 standards to ensure compliance and information security.

## Refund Criteria

Refunds may be granted under the following circumstances:

- Event cancellation by the company.
- Significant changes to the event schedule or content that impact the participant’s ability to attend.
- Valid personal emergencies, subject to documentation and approval.

Refunds will not be granted for:

- No-shows at the event.
- Cancellation requests made after the specified refund request deadline.
- Circumstances explicitly excluded in the event's terms and conditions.

## Refund Process

1. **Submission**: Participants must submit a refund request through the designated online form or email address no later than the specified deadline.
2. **Review**: The request will be reviewed by the event coordination team to determine its eligibility based on the criteria outlined in this policy.
3. **Communication**: The participant will be notified of the decision within five business days.
4. **Processing**: Approved refunds will be processed within 10 business days via the original payment method.

## Responsibilities

- **Event Organizers**: Ensure participants are informed of the refund policy at the time of registration and manage refund requests in a timely manner.
- **Participants**: Understand and adhere to the terms and conditions of the refund policy when registering for events.
- **Policy Owner (Ryan Laird)**: Oversee the implementation, review, and updates of this policy in alignment with organizational goals and standards.
