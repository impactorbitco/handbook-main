## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines and standards for managing intellectual property rights (IPR) within the organization, ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations, and aligning with the ISO/IEC 27001: Information Security Management System framework. This policy supports the strategic direction of the organization as part of its commitment to information security and proper governance of intellectual property.

## Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who create, access, or manage intellectual property on behalf of the organization. It encompasses all forms of intellectual property, including but not limited to copyrights, patents, trademarks, trade secrets, and any other proprietary information.

## Policy Statement

The organization is committed to the protection, management, and enforcement of intellectual property rights to safeguard its assets and support innovation. All employees and stakeholders are required to adhere to this policy to ensure that intellectual property is handled responsibly and in compliance with legal and organizational standards.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Owner

The owner of this policy, Ryan Laird, is responsible for its maintenance, updates, and ensuring adherence throughout the organization.

### Employees and Contractors

All individuals working for or on behalf of the organization are responsible for understanding and complying with the intellectual property rights policy. This includes reporting any suspected misuse or infringement of intellectual property.

### Leadership

Leadership is responsible for promoting awareness of this policy and ensuring that adequate resources are allocated for its implementation and enforcement.

## Key Principles

1. **Protection of Intellectual Property**: All intellectual property created by employees or contractors during their course of work for the organization is owned by the organization, unless otherwise specified by contractual agreements.
2. **Compliance with Legal and Regulatory Standards**: The organization will comply with all applicable intellectual property laws and regulations, including those outlined in the ISO/IEC 27001: Information Security Management System framework.
3. **Confidentiality and Security**: Intellectual property must be treated as confidential and securely stored to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
4. **Proper Attribution**: The organization will ensure proper acknowledgment and attribution of intellectual property to its rightful owners when required.
5. **Training and Awareness**: Employees and contractors will be provided with relevant training and resources to understand their responsibilities under this policy.
