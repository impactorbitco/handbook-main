## Introduction

### Purpose
This Code of Ethics outlines the principles and standards that guide the professional conduct of everyone at Impact Orbit Creative Group. It provides a framework to ensure our operations, partnerships, and projects are conducted with integrity, transparency, and respect for people, society, and the environment.  

### Scope
This Code applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, volunteers, and any individual acting on behalf of Impact Orbit Creative Group, including:  
- Green Orbit Digital  
- Green Orbit Space Communications & PR  
- Green Orbit Academy  

### Why Ethics Matter
Ethical behaviour builds trust, credibility, and long-term success. Acting ethically protects our reputation, strengthens relationships with clients and partners, and ensures our work contributes positively to the space sector and wider communities.  

### Core Values
- **Integrity:** Act honestly and consistently with our values.  
- **Respect:** Foster an inclusive, supportive, and harassment-free environment.  
- **Accountability:** Take responsibility for decisions and actions.  
- **Sustainability:** Make decisions that protect the environment and society.  
- **Excellence:** Deliver high-quality work responsibly and professionally.  

---

## Ethical Conduct

### Independence and Impartiality
- Avoid external influence that may compromise judgment.  
- Make decisions free from bias or pressure.  

### Honesty and Professionalism
- Communicate truthfully, clearly, and respectfully.  
- Deliver services with competence and professionalism.  
- Avoid actions that could damage the companyâ€™s reputation.  

### Compliance with Laws
- Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards.  
- Comply with local and international requirements, including data protection, employment, and sustainability laws.  

### Conflicts of Interest
- Disclose potential conflicts to management promptly.  
- Avoid using your position for personal advantage.  
- Follow internal policies for gifts, hospitality, and external engagements.  

### Protecting Company Resources
- Use physical, intellectual, and digital assets responsibly.  
- Protect confidential information, including client data and business plans.  
- Ensure all records are accurate, secure, and compliant with regulations.  

### Upholding Reputation
- Represent the company consistently with its values.  
- Use digital media and public communication responsibly.  
- Maintain integrity in partnerships, sponsorships, and collaborations.  

### Respectful Behaviour
- Promote equality, diversity, and inclusion.  
- Prevent harassment, discrimination, bullying, or abuse of authority.  
- Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with fairness and respect.  

### Substance Use
- Avoid illegal drugs or alcohol while performing professional duties.  
- Follow workplace safety policies and standards.  

---

## Obligations and Consequences

### Duty to Comply
All individuals must understand and adhere to this Code. Supervisors and managers must ensure compliance and provide guidance where necessary.  

### Consequences of Non-Compliance
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action, including termination, and legal action depending on severity.  

### Reporting Misconduct
- Report suspected ethical violations or unsafe practices through designated channels.  
- Retaliation against those reporting concerns in good faith is strictly prohibited.  
- See [Whistleblowing and Non-Retaliation Policy](https://www.notion.so/Whistleblowing-and-Non-Retaliation-Policy-1c0d6625c67981158500cdbb5c9045ef?pvs=21) for guidance.  

---

## Ethical Decision-Making
1. Assess the situation in light of company values and applicable laws.  
2. Consider impacts on colleagues, clients, partners, and the wider community.  
3. Seek guidance if uncertain.  
4. Make transparent, fair, and sustainable decisions.  

---

## Sustainability and Corporate Responsibility
- Minimise environmental impact in all activities.  
- Promote sustainability internally and with partners.  
- Integrate sustainability into strategic and operational decisions.  

---

## Partnerships and External Relations
- Conduct due diligence on partners to ensure ethical alignment.  
- Avoid relationships with entities engaging in bribery, corruption, or human rights abuses.  
- Maintain transparency and accountability in contracts and collaborations.
