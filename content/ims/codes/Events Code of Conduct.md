## 1. Purpose and Scope
Impact Orbit Creative Group is committed to creating inclusive, respectful, and safe environments where all participants can engage fully and equitably.  

This Code of Conduct applies to **all Impact Orbit and Green Orbit Digital events**, both in-person and online. It ensures a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all participants, speakers, staff, and volunteers.  

Our events follow high ethical and professional standards, and all participants are expected to behave with integrity and respect toward everyone attending or involved in our events.

---

## 2. Applicability
This Code of Conduct applies to all Impact Orbit events, including but not limited to:  
- Conferences, symposiums, workshops, seminars, and panels  
- Online meetings, webinars, and virtual conferences  
- Exhibitions, receptions, networking events, and community activities  
- Any gathering organised, hosted, sponsored, or supported in whole or in part by Impact Orbit  

The Code applies to **all participants** in any capacity, including registered attendees, speakers, facilitators, exhibitors, sponsors, volunteers, partners, and staff.  

This Code supplements other applicable organisational policies, laws, and venue regulations. It does not replace legal or regulatory requirements.

---

## 3. Expected Behaviour
Participants are expected to:  
- Treat everyone with respect, courtesy, and consideration  
- Communicate openly and thoughtfully, valuing diverse perspectives and experiences  
- Be mindful of your surroundings and fellow participants  
- Support an environment where all participants feel welcome and able to contribute  
- Address and include all participants equally during discussions, Q&A sessions, and workshops  
- Alert event organisers if you notice violations of this Code  
- Maintain professionalism in all interactions, including online and social media communications  
- Respect privacy and intellectual property of other participants  
- Assume positive intent and respond to misunderstandings constructively  

---

## 4. Prohibited Conduct
The following behaviours are considered harassment or misconduct and are prohibited:  

### 4.1 Harassment
Harassment is any improper or unwelcome conduct that might reasonably be expected to cause offence, discomfort, or humiliation. Prohibited harassment includes behaviour based on:  
- Gender, gender identity or expression  
- Sexual orientation  
- Ethnicity, race, nationality, or cultural background  
- Religion or belief  
- Age  
- Disability or medical condition  
- Physical appearance  
- Political affiliation  
- Family or caregiving responsibilities  
- Any other protected characteristic  

Harassment may be verbal, nonverbal, physical, written, or digital.

### 4.2 Sexual Harassment
Sexual harassment includes any unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature. Examples include:  
- Sexualised comments about appearance, clothing, or body parts  
- Derogatory remarks about sexual orientation or gender identity  
- Repeated unwelcome invitations for dates or sexual activity  
- Sexually suggestive staring or gestures  
- Unwelcome touching, including brushing, patting, or pinching  
- Sharing sexual anecdotes, jokes, images, or videos  
- Sending sexually explicit messages  
- Attempted or actual sexual assault  

### 4.3 Bullying
Bullying includes behaviour that demeans, intimidates, humiliates, or sabotages an individual or group, whether persistent or a single severe incident.

### 4.4 Other Unacceptable Conduct
- Sustained disruption of talks or events  
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following  
- Unauthorized photography or recording  
- Aggressive, discriminatory, or exclusionary behaviour  
- Any conduct that violates venue rules, platform rules, or applicable laws  

---

## 5. Event Guidelines

### 5.1 Online Events
- Use your real name for identification unless otherwise specified  
- Keep your microphone muted when not speaking  
- Use the designated channels for questions and comments  
- Do not share meeting links or access credentials with unauthorized persons  
- Respect participants' privacy by not taking screenshots or recordings without explicit permission  

### 5.2 In-Person Events
- Wear your event badge/identification at all times  
- Follow venue safety and security protocols  
- Respect the physical distancing and personal space preferences of others  
- Follow organisers' instructions regarding photography and recording  
- Report any unsafe situations or concerning behaviour immediately to staff  

---

## 6. Accessibility and Inclusion
In alignment with our Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion (EDI) Policy, we are committed to making our events accessible to everyone.  
Participants requiring accommodations should contact the event organisers **at least 7 days in advance**.

---

## 7. Safety, Reporting, and Support
Participants should report unsafe situations or breaches of this Code immediately. Reports can be made:  
- Directly to event organisers, a designated safeguarding contact, or responsible staff member  
- For urgent issues, call the emergency contact provided for the event  
- Via email: [support@impactorbit.co](mailto:support@impactorbit.co)  

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. Knowingly submitting false or misleading allegations is prohibited.

---

## 8. Consequences of Misconduct
Event organisers may take any action necessary to maintain a safe and respectful environment. This may include:  
- Verbal warning  
- Temporary or permanent expulsion from the event  
- Removal from sessions or the entire event without refund  
- Reporting to appropriate authorities  
- Suspension or revocation of future attendance privileges  

Participants asked to stop inappropriate behaviour must comply immediately.

---

## 9. Protection Against Retaliation
Threats, intimidation, or retaliation against anyone who reports a concern or participates in an investigation are strictly prohibited. Measures will be taken to prevent and address retaliation.

---

## 10. Acknowledgment
By participating in Impact Orbit Creative Group and Green Orbit Digital events, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.  

We thank you for helping create a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment for all participants.
