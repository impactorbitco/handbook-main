## Purpose
The Code of Confidentiality establishes the principles and responsibilities for protecting sensitive and proprietary information at Impact Orbit Creative Group. It ensures that employees, contractors, and associates understand the importance of confidentiality and act consistently to protect the interests of our clients, partners, and the company.  

---

## Scope
This Code applies to all individuals associated with Impact Orbit Creative Group, including:  
- Employees  
- Board members and investors  
- Contractors and consultants  
- Volunteers and temporary staff  

It covers all divisions, including Green Orbit Digital, Green Orbit Space Communications & PR, and Green Orbit Academy.

---

## Definition of Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:  
- Intellectual property (trade secrets, patents, copyrights)  
- Business plans, strategies, and financial data  
- Client, partner, and vendor information  
- Employee records and personal information  
- Technical data, software, and proprietary processes  
- Information shared under Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)  
- Documents marked as confidential or proprietary  

---

## Duty of Confidentiality
All personnel have a duty to maintain the confidentiality of information obtained during their engagement with Impact Orbit Creative Group. This obligation extends indefinitely, including after the termination of employment or engagement.

---

## Non-Disclosure Obligations
- Confidential information must not be disclosed to any third party without prior authorisation.  
- Disclosure is only permitted where required by law or approved by senior management.  
- Confidential information must not be used for personal gain or advantage.  

---

## Access and Handling
- Access to confidential information is limited to individuals with a need-to-know.  
- Physical documents must be stored securely and electronic files protected with passwords and encryption.  
- Sensitive data transmitted electronically must be encrypted.  
- Documents no longer required should be securely shredded or deleted.  
- Employees must safeguard login credentials and prevent unauthorised access to systems.  

---

## Confidentiality Agreements
Employees, contractors, and associates may be required to sign NDAs or specific confidentiality agreements for projects or engagements involving particularly sensitive information.

---

## Reporting Breaches
- Suspected or actual breaches must be reported immediately to a manager, supervisor, or designated security officer.  
- Prompt reporting ensures risks are mitigated and corrective actions taken.  

---

## Training and Awareness
Impact Orbit Creative Group provides ongoing training and awareness programmes to ensure all personnel understand their responsibilities regarding confidentiality and are equipped to manage sensitive information appropriately.

---

## Compliance
All personnel must comply with this Code, internal policies, and applicable laws and regulations governing the protection of confidential information, including data protection and industry-specific standards.

---

## Consequences of Violations
Violations of this Code may result in:  
- Disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract  
- Legal consequences, including civil liability or criminal prosecution  

---

## Acknowledgment
By accepting employment or engagement with Impact Orbit Creative Group, individuals acknowledge their understanding of, and agreement to comply with, this Code of Confidentiality.
